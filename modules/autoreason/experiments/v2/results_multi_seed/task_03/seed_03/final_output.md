# Hybrid Work Policy
**Effective Date:** [Date]  
**Policy Version:** 1.0  
**Review Cycle:** Quarterly

## Executive Summary

This policy establishes a pragmatic hybrid work model that accommodates different team needs while maintaining operational effectiveness and legal compliance. The framework prioritizes business outcomes over attendance requirements and provides clear, enforceable guidelines.

## Core Framework

### Role-Based Work Arrangements

**Engineering Team (20 people)**
- **Office requirement:** One team collaboration day per quarter (advance notice provided)
- **Additional requirement:** Quarterly all-company meeting (total: 4 office days annually)
- **Business rationale:** Engineering work is primarily individual/small team focused with deliverables that can be measured remotely
- **Flexibility:** Full remote work between required sessions

**Sales Team (15 people)**  
- **Office requirement:** Two days per week (Tuesday/Wednesday - core collaboration days)
- **Additional requirement:** Quarterly all-company meeting
- **Business rationale:** Sales requires frequent client meetings, deal coordination, and pipeline management that benefits from in-person interaction
- **Flexibility:** Monday, Thursday, Friday flexible (remote or office)

**Leadership & Support Functions (15 people)**
- **Office requirement:** Wednesday (company coordination day) plus one additional day
- **Additional requirement:** Quarterly all-company meeting
- **Business rationale:** Cross-functional coordination and decision-making benefits from regular in-person interaction

### Universal Requirements

**Quarterly All-Company Meeting**
- One day per quarter, all US-based employees in office
- EU team participates virtually with dedicated 2-hour morning session (8-10 AM ET / 2-4 PM CET)
- Scheduled 6 months in advance for planning purposes

## Meeting Norms & Communication Standards

### Meeting Guidelines

**Timezone-Conscious Scheduling**
- **US East/West coordination:** 11 AM - 1 PM ET (8 AM - 10 AM PT)
- **EU inclusion meetings:** Tuesday/Wednesday 8:30 AM - 9:30 AM ET (2:30 PM - 3:30 PM CET)
- **Engineering standups:** Asynchronous via Slack with weekly video sync
- **Sales meetings:** 10 AM ET (accommodates West Coast, excludes EU for daily tactical discussions)

**Hybrid Meeting Standards**
- Default to video-first for all meetings
- Hybrid meetings use dedicated conference room with professional AV (no individual device requirement for in-office participants)
- Meeting notes required for all decisions and action items
- 24-hour advance notice required for meeting changes

### Communication Framework

**Primary Tools**
- **Real-time:** Slack for immediate communication
- **Documentation:** Google Workspace (Docs, Sheets, Drive)
- **Project management:** Existing CRM + Monday.com for task tracking

**Response Expectations**
- **Critical issues (defined as system outages, security breaches, or customer escalations requiring immediate action):** 4 hours during local business hours only
- **Standard business:** 24 hours during local business hours
- **EU team coordination:** Twice weekly scheduled check-ins (Tuesday/Thursday 8:30 AM ET)

## Equipment & Technology Policy

### Equipment Provision

**All Remote/Hybrid Employees**
- Laptop upgrade if current device >3 years old
- Noise-canceling headphones for calls
- $40/month internet/phone stipend (US employees only)

**High-Remote Employees (>50% remote work)**
- External monitor and ergonomic accessories up to $400 per person
- Applies to all 20 engineering employees and qualifying sales/support staff

**EU Equipment Handling**
- EU employees receive equivalent budget (€400) for local equipment purchase
- Employees submit receipts for reimbursement within 30 days
- Company IT provides setup support via video call
- EU employees responsible for customs/import duties on company-shipped items

**Office Infrastructure**
- Utilize existing conference room equipment
- Upgrade one conference room for hybrid meetings with professional AV setup
- Maintain hot-desking stations with basic peripherals

### IT Support

**Security Requirements**
- Company device management software (existing)
- VPN required for all remote work
- Multi-factor authentication mandatory

**Support Structure**
- IT helpdesk during business hours (8 AM - 6 PM ET)
- Loaner laptops available for US employees (office pickup required)
- EU employees receive expedited repair/replacement budget (€200 emergency fund)

## Performance Evaluation Framework

### Objective-Based Assessment

**Universal Metrics**
- **Results delivery:** Achievement of quarterly goals/OKRs (60%)
- **Work quality:** Manager assessment based on deliverable quality and accuracy (40%)

**Team-Specific Metrics**

**Engineering:**
- Story point completion and code quality (measured via existing code review process)
- Technical documentation contributions (tracked in company wiki)
- Code review participation (measured in Git commits)

**Sales:**
- Revenue targets, pipeline development, customer satisfaction scores
- Deal cycle efficiency and forecast accuracy
- CRM data completeness (automated scoring)

### Review Process

**Quarterly Reviews**
- Manager 1:1 with structured goal assessment
- Self-assessment focused on quarterly objectives
- Development planning for next quarter

**Performance Support**
- Bi-weekly 1:1s with direct manager (minimum 30 minutes)
- Monthly team retrospectives
- Access to professional development budget ($1,000/year per employee)

## EU Team Integration Strategy

### Practical Timezone Management

**EU Team Members (3 people)**
- **Coordination meetings:** Tuesday/Thursday 8:30-9:30 AM ET (2:30-3:30 PM CET)
- **Asynchronous participation:** All US meetings recorded with 24-hour async input window
- **Quarterly engagement:** 2-hour virtual session during company meetings

**Travel Policy**
- EU team members visit US office once annually for strategic planning (optional)
- Travel budget: $3,000 per EU employee per year
- All international travel requires 90-day advance planning and budget approval

### Workflow Coordination

**Handoff Protocols**
- Engineering: End-of-day status updates in Slack, morning review by US team
- Sales: Weekly pipeline review calls (Tuesday 8:30 AM ET)
- Support: Clear escalation procedures documented in company wiki

## Implementation Timeline

### Month 1-2: Foundation and Pilot
- Policy communication and Q&A sessions
- Pilot hybrid meetings with sales team only
- Essential equipment orders placed (delivery 4-6 weeks)
- IT security setup completion

### Month 3-4: Phased Rollout
- Begin office attendance requirements for sales team
- Engineering quarterly meeting scheduled
- Equipment delivery and setup support
- Collect pilot feedback and adjust protocols

### Month 5-6: Full Implementation
- All teams following office requirements
- Complete first quarterly reviews under new system
- First quarterly all-company meeting
- Full equipment rollout completed

## Compliance and Enforcement

### Attendance Monitoring
- Office badge data tracked for compliance reporting (employees notified of tracking)
- Exceptions require manager approval with business justification
- Consistent non-compliance addressed through standard performance management process

### Legal Compliance
- EU team working hours: maximum 40 hours/week in compliance with local labor laws
- Equipment reimbursement follows local tax regulations (employees responsible for tax implications)
- Remote work agreements updated to reflect policy requirements
- GDPR compliance for any EU employee data tracking

## Success Metrics

### Quantitative Measures
- **Employee retention:** <15% annual turnover

- **Cost management:** Total policy implementation cost tracking against budget

### Qualitative Assessment
- Quarterly pulse survey on work arrangement satisfaction (anonymous)
- Manager feedback on team collaboration effectiveness
- Customer satisfaction scores maintained or improved
