# Remote Work Policy: Adaptive Hybrid Framework for [Company Name]

**Effective Date:** [Date]  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Review Cycle:** Quarterly for first year, then annually

## Executive Summary

This policy establishes an adaptive hybrid work model that honors team-specific operational needs while ensuring company cohesion and equitable treatment. Engineering maintains remote-first autonomy, Sales achieves collaboration-intensive goals, and all functions benefit from unified performance standards, comprehensive equipment support, and thoughtful global coordination across US East, US West, and EU timezones.

---

## 1. In-Office Requirements and Capacity Framework

### 1.1 Universal Foundation (All Teams)
- **Baseline Requirement:** 1 day per month minimum for all employees
- **Company All-Hands:** Monthly (hybrid format with recordings for EU accommodation)
- **Quarterly Planning:** All-hands required in-person (travel budget for EU team)

### 1.2 Function-Specific Requirements

**Engineering Teams (20 people) - Remote-First Model:**
- **Core Requirement:** Quarterly 2-day collaborative sprints (8 additional days/year)
- **Optional Office Access:** Tuesday-Thursday with 24-hour booking
- **Team Autonomy:** Daily standups and coordination methods determined by team

**Sales Teams (15 people) - Collaboration-First Model:**
- **Core Requirement:** 3 days per week (Tuesday-Thursday)
- **Structured Collaboration:** One designated team day weekly for shared activities
- **Client Flexibility:** Additional office days as needed for local client meetings
- **Core Hours:** 10 AM - 4 PM ET on in-office days

**Other Functions (15 people) - Balanced Hybrid:**
- **Core Requirement:** 2 days per week (choose from Tuesday-Thursday)
- **Team Coordination:** Each function designates one shared monthly team day
- **Individual Assessment:** Case-by-case flexibility for specific role needs

### 1.3 Smart Capacity Management
- **Office Capacity:** 35 desks (70% of headcount) with intelligent allocation
- **Booking System:** 24-hour advance booking with automated waitlist
- **Priority Allocation:**
  - Sales: 15 desks reserved Tuesday-Thursday
  - Engineering Sprints: Full office access during quarterly sprint weeks
  - General Pool: 20 desks for flexible booking
- **Capacity Analytics:** Monthly utilization review with adjustment protocols

## 2. Meeting Standards and Communication Excellence

### 2.1 Global Collaboration Windows
- **Primary Core Hours:** 12 PM - 3 PM ET / 9 AM - 12 PM PT / 5 PM - 8 PM CET
- **Secondary US Window:** 9 AM - 12 PM ET / 6 AM - 9 AM PT (when EU participation not required)
- **EU Protection:** No recurring meetings after 6 PM CET; Friday afternoons (post 3 PM CET) protected

### 2.2 Hybrid-Excellence Meeting Framework
**Technology Standards:**
- All meetings default to video-enabled with professional dial-in options
- In-office attendees use designated conference rooms with high-quality AV equipment
- Meeting organizer accountable for equal remote participant engagement
- Real-time collaborative tools (shared screens, digital whiteboards) standard

**Meeting Types and Cadence:**
- **Daily Coordination:** Team preference (async updates encouraged for Engineering)
- **Weekly Team Meetings:** Hybrid format during core collaboration hours
- **Monthly All-Hands:** Hybrid with in-office catering and full recording with timestamps
- **Quarterly Planning:** In-person required (EU team: 1-2 attend annually in rotation)

### 2.3 Communication Protocol and Response Standards
- **Platform Hierarchy:** Slack (async), Zoom (sync), Notion (documentation)
- **Response Expectations:**
  - Urgent matters: 2-4 hours during business hours (timezone buffer included)
  - Standard requests: 24 hours during business days
  - Cross-timezone coordination: 48 hours maximum
- **Decision Documentation:** All significant decisions recorded in Notion within 24 hours
- **EU Integration:** No major decisions without EU input or 48-hour review period

## 7. Conflict Resolution and Exception Framework

### 7.1 Proactive Conflict Prevention
**Capacity and Scheduling:**
- Automated waitlist system with 24-hour advance notifications
- Department head mediation for team-level conflicts with HR support
- Manager discretion for individual conflicts with HR consultation and documentation

### 7.2 Exception Management and Accommodation
**Individual Accommodations:**
- Streamlined HR review process within 3 business days
- 30-day trial periods with clear success criteria and regular check-ins
- Permanent accommodation pathway with performance validation

**Team-Level Modifications:**
- Business case requirement for department-wide changes
- CEO approval for function-wide policy modifications
- 60-day pilot periods with comprehensive impact assessment
- Success criteria definition and measurement protocols

### 7.3 Escalation Excellence
**Resolution Pathway:**
1. Direct manager discussion and initial problem-solving
2. HR consultation and mediation support
3. Department head review and decision authority
4. CEO final decision for complex or precedent-setting cases

**Timeline Commitment:** Maximum 10 business days for complete resolution with interim solutions provided for urgent matters
