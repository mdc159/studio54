**MEMORANDUM**

**TO:** All Employees  
**FROM:** Executive Leadership  
**DATE:** [Date]  
**RE:** Artificial Intelligence Tool Usage Policy

---

## SCOPE

1. This policy applies to all 200 employees across engineering, sales, and support functions.
2. Covers all AI-powered tools used for work purposes, including code generation, content creation, and data analysis.
3. Supersedes informal AI usage practices currently in place.

## PERMITTED USES

1. GitHub Copilot Business for code completion and generation within assigned development environments.
2. AI assistance for internal documentation, meeting notes, and non-customer-facing communications.
3. Code review and debugging assistance using approved tools with sanitized, non-proprietary code samples.
4. Sales email drafting and content creation for prospects using original company materials as source material only.

## PROHIBITED USES

1. Input of customer PII, financial data, or database schemas into any AI service, motivated by SOC2, GDPR obligations, and existing DPA violations (incident #1).
2. Use of AI-generated content containing third-party copyrighted material without verification and attribution, motivated by copyright infringement risk (incident #2).
3. Committing AI-generated code without reviewing and removing incompatible license headers or GPL-licensed components, motivated by licensing conflicts (incident #3).
4. Use of non-approved AI tools that lack enterprise data protection agreements, motivated by pending FedRAMP authorization requirements.
5. Processing EU customer data through AI services without explicit GDPR-compliant data processing agreements.
6. Relying on AI-generated code for IP protection or patent applications, motivated by non-copyrightability of AI-generated works.

## ENFORCEMENT

1. Engineering managers will conduct monthly code review audits for license compliance and customer data exposure.
2. Sales leadership will review AI-assisted customer communications quarterly for copyright and accuracy issues.
3. IT will monitor and block access to non-approved AI services through existing network controls.
4. First violation results in mandatory training and 30-day monitoring period.
5. Second violation results in written warning and removal of AI tool access for 90 days.
6. Third violation results in disciplinary action up to termination.
7. Immediate termination for intentional customer data exposure through AI services.
8. All employees must complete AI usage training within 30 days of policy effective date.

Questions regarding this policy should be directed to your manager or the Legal team.
