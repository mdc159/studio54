Go-to-Market Strategy: Kubernetes Config CLI

1. Target Customer
Segment: Mid-market platform engineering teams at cloud-native companies with 200-1000 employees
- Typical profile: Kubernetes environments with 10-50 clusters, struggling with configuration management complexity
- Pain point: Manual YAML configuration sprawl consuming 8-12 hours/week per senior engineer
- Budget: $500-1500/month for tools that reduce operational toil
- Why now: Increasing Kubernetes adoption (49% of enterprises in 2022) and growing complexity of multi-cluster management

2. Pricing
Tier: Pro Team @ $750/month
- Includes 10 user seats, unlimited clusters
- Justification: Saves 40 engineering hours/month at fully-loaded senior engineer rate ($150/hour)
- Clear ROI: Potential $6,000/month value for $750 investment
- Annual prepay option with 20% discount to incentivize commitment

3. Distribution
Primary Channel: Technical Community Acquisition
- Targeted sponsorship of 3 top Kubernetes podcasts (KubeCast, Cloud Native FM)
- Speak at 2 regional DevOps conferences (KubeCon, DevOpsDays)
- Produce deep-dive technical content on configuration management challenges
- Leverage existing 5k GitHub stars for credibility and social proof
- Goal: 500 qualified leads in first 6 months

4. First 6 Months Milestones
1. Product-Market Fit (Month 3)
- 50 paid beta customers
- 80% retention rate
- Average usage of 3+ clusters per customer

2. Sales Validation (Month 5)
- $75,000 annual recurring revenue
- 3 enterprise proof-of-concept conversations
- Net Promoter Score > 60

3. Channel Expansion (Month 6)
- First affiliate partnerships with 2 Kubernetes consulting firms
- Integration with 3 major cloud marketplaces
- Documented customer case study

5. What We Won't Do
- No enterprise sales team in first 12 months
- No multi-language support beyond core use cases
- No custom enterprise feature development before product-market fit

6. Biggest Risk
Risk: Competitors launching similar solutions
Mitigation: Rapid innovation cycle with monthly feature releases
Metric to Watch: GitHub stars growth rate (target: 20%+ monthly)

Appendix: Competitive Landscape
- Direct competitors charge $500-1000/month
- Our pricing positions us as premium but accessible
- Differentiation through deep technical community engagement

Tactical Execution Principles:
- Technical credibility over marketing hype
- Community-driven growth
- Clear, measurable value proposition
