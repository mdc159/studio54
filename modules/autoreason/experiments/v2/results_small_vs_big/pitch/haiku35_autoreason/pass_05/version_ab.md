Go-to-Market Strategy for Kubernetes Config CLI

1. Target Customer
Primary Segment: Enterprise B2B platform engineering teams in cloud-native companies
- Profile: 100-500 employee tech companies managing 10-25 Kubernetes clusters
- Pain Point: Configuration drift causing 40% of unexpected production incidents
- Budget: $750-1,500/month for infrastructure management tools
- Why Now: 62% of enterprises report Kubernetes complexity as primary scaling blocker (CNCF Survey 2022)

Unique Value Proposition:
- Real-time drift detection
- Multi-cluster state synchronization
- Native support for complex configuration inheritance

2. Pricing
Tier: Pro Team @ $549/month
- 5 user seats, up to 20 clusters
- ROI Calculation:
  * Prevents 1 major configuration incident per quarter
  * Average incident cost: $17,000 (Ponemon Institute)
  * Direct savings: $68,000/year
  * Tool cost: $6,588/year
  * Net ROI: 932%
- Positioned 10% below market competitors

4. First 6 Months Milestones
1. Product Validation (Month 3)
- 20 paid customers with 3+ clusters

- 80% customer satisfaction

2. Revenue Validation (Month 5)

3. Community Expansion (Month 6)

5. What We Won't Do
- No enterprise sales team in first 12 months
- No custom integrations beyond top 3 cloud platforms
- No manual onboarding processes

Mitigation:

- Create transparent feature comparison
- Implement 30-day full-feature trial with usage analytics
Critical Metrics:

- Pivot trigger: <0.5% conversion after 4 months
