**Revised Go-to-Market Strategy for Kubernetes Config CLI**

* **Budget:** $20,000 to $50,000 per annum, allocated to tooling and automation (Source: Forrester, 2022)
* **Why now:** As Kubernetes adoption accelerates, the need for efficient config management grows exponentially, and SKAs are under pressure to optimize cluster performance.
* **Why our tool:** Our CLI addresses the specific pain points of SKAs, offering a faster, more accurate, and scalable solution with features like automated config drift detection and remediation.
