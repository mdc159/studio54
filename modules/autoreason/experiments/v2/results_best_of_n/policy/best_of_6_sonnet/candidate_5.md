**MEMORANDUM**

TO: All Employees
FROM: Executive Team
DATE: [Current Date]
RE: Artificial Intelligence Tool Usage Policy

This policy governs the use of AI tools by all employees to protect customer data, intellectual property, and regulatory compliance while enabling productivity gains.

## SCOPE

1. This policy applies to all 200 employees across engineering, sales, and other departments.
2. AI tools include any software that uses machine learning to generate code, text, images, or other content.
3. This policy covers both company-provided and personal AI tools used for work purposes.
4. All AI tool usage must comply with existing SOC2 Type II, GDPR, and pending FedRAMP requirements.

## PERMITTED USES

1. GitHub Copilot Business for code generation and completion (80 licensed seats available).
2. AI coding assistants for writing new code that does not contain customer data, proprietary algorithms, or sensitive business logic.
3. AI tools for generating marketing copy, email templates, and sales materials that do not reference competitors or third-party intellectual property.
4. AI tools for internal documentation, meeting summaries, and process improvement materials.
5. Use of AI-generated content only after human review and approval by the responsible team lead.

## PROHIBITED USES

1. Inputting customer PII or financial data into any AI service (violates DPA terms and GDPR obligations).
2. Pasting database schemas, API specifications, or system architecture into AI tools (based on Q1 security incident).
3. Using AI-generated text that includes competitor content without verification (based on Q2 compliance incident).
4. Committing AI-generated code without reviewing and removing any third-party license headers or attributions (based on Q3 legal incident).
5. Using personal AI accounts or unauthorized AI services for work-related tasks.
6. Inputting proprietary algorithms, security configurations, or confidential business information into AI tools.

## ENFORCEMENT

1. Engineering managers will review code commits for unauthorized AI-generated content during existing pull request processes.
2. Sales managers will review AI-assisted customer communications before sending during current approval workflows.
3. IT will monitor and block access to unauthorized AI services through existing network controls.
4. Violations will result in immediate suspension of AI tool access and disciplinary action up to termination.
5. Department heads will conduct monthly spot-checks of AI tool usage within their teams.
6. All employees must acknowledge this policy within 30 days and complete annual compliance training.

Questions regarding this policy should be directed to your department head or the Legal team.
