AI Tool Usage Policy Memo
Effective Date: [Current Date]

Scope:
1. This policy applies to all employees of [Company Name], including full-time, part-time, and contract workers.
2. The policy covers all AI tools and generative AI services used for work-related tasks.
3. The policy applies to company-owned and personal devices used for work purposes.
4. This policy is mandatory and supersedes any previous informal practices.

Permitted Uses:
1. GitHub Copilot Business is the only approved AI coding assistant for software development.
2. AI tools may be used for:
   a. Code completion and suggestion
   b. Documentation generation
   c. Technical problem-solving
   d. Non-sensitive email drafting
3. Users must review and validate all AI-generated content before use.
4. AI tool usage must comply with existing software development and communication standards.
5. Employees may request additional AI tools through the IT procurement process, subject to security and compliance review.

Prohibited Uses:
1. Inputting customer Personally Identifiable Information (PII) into any AI service (Ref: Base Fact - Incident 1, Compliance Requirements)
2. Sharing or generating code containing:
   a. Copyrighted material
   b. Open-source software with restrictive licensing headers
   c. Potentially non-copyrightable generated content (Ref: Legal Review)
3. Using AI tools for:
   a. Generating entire documents or code without human review
   b. Handling sensitive financial or customer data
   c. Producing final deliverables without manual verification
4. Accessing AI services not explicitly approved by the IT department.
5. Using personal AI accounts for work-related tasks.
6. Bypassing existing data protection and confidentiality agreements.

Enforcement:
1. Violations will result in progressive disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
2. Quarterly audits will be conducted to ensure compliance:
   a. Code repositories will be scanned for AI-generated content
   b. Communication channels will be monitored for inappropriate AI tool usage
3. Managers are responsible for:
   a. Communicating this policy to their teams
   b. Reporting potential violations
   c. Ensuring team adherence
4. Employees must complete mandatory AI usage training within 30 days of policy implementation.
5. IT and Legal departments will maintain a current list of approved and prohibited AI tools.
