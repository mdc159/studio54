MEMO: Company AI Tool Usage Policy
Effective Date: [Current Date]

SCOPE:
1. This policy applies to all employees across all departments.
2. Covers all generative AI tools, including but not limited to large language models, code generation, and text/image synthesis platforms.
3. Applies to company-owned and personal devices when used for work purposes.
4. Supersedes all previous informal practices related to AI tool usage.

PERMITTED USES:
1. GitHub Copilot Business is the only approved AI coding assistant for engineering teams.
2. AI tools may be used for:
   a. Code completion and refactoring
   b. Technical documentation generation
   c. Email drafting and communication enhancement
3. Users must maintain final review and responsibility for all AI-generated content.
4. All AI-generated work must be substantively edited and verified by the human user.
5. Sales teams may use AI for email template generation and communication optimization within approved company tools.

PROHIBITED USES:
1. Inputting any customer Personal Identifiable Information (PII) into external AI platforms (Ref: Base Fact #2, Incident #1).
2. Generating or committing code with third-party library licenses without explicit legal review (Ref: Base Fact #2, Incident #3).
3. Using AI tools to generate complete documents or code without human verification.
4. Copying verbatim text or code from AI-generated outputs without independent review.
5. Using personal or unauthorized AI tools for work-related tasks.
6. Inputting proprietary company or customer financial data into external AI services (Ref: Base Fact #3, Legal Review).
7. Representing AI-generated content as original human work.

ENFORCEMENT:
1. Violations will result in progressive disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
2. Engineering managers must conduct monthly code review audits checking for AI-generated content compliance.
3. Quarterly training sessions will be mandatory for all employees on AI tool usage policies.
4. Employees must report any potential policy violations to their direct manager or HR immediately.
5. Compliance will be tracked through existing performance review and code review processes.
6. The company reserves the right to audit AI tool usage logs and generated content.
