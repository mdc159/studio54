The task asks for a policy memo, not a revision log. This preamble content is filler that does not belong in the deliverable and inflates the effective word count ambiguity.

The policy memo section alone (from "AI Tool Usage Policy" through the final Enforcement item) runs approximately 520–540 words. The constraint is a hard maximum of 500 words. Even excluding the preamble, the memo itself appears to violate this constraint.

### 3. Permitted Uses Item 2 Is Circular and Unenforceable as Written
Item 2 under Permitted Uses states AI-drafted communications are permitted "subject to the human review requirement in Enforcement item 2." A permitted use that is defined entirely by a cross-reference to an enforcement condition provides no standalone guidance. More critically, it does not specify *what* is permitted — any AI tool?
