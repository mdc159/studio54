# Go-to-Market Strategy: Kubernetes CLI Configuration Tool

## Executive Summary

This GTM strategy focuses on converting your existing open-source momentum into sustainable revenue through a freemium SaaS model targeting DevOps engineers and platform teams. The approach prioritizes direct engagement with your GitHub community while building enterprise features that justify paid tiers.

## Target Customer Segments

### Primary: Mid-Market DevOps Teams (50-500 employees)
**Profile**: Companies with 5-20 engineers managing multiple Kubernetes clusters
- **Pain Points**: Configuration drift, lack of standardization, manual deployment processes
- **Budget Authority**: Engineering managers with $10-50k annual tooling budgets
- **Decision Timeline**: 2-4 weeks for tools under $25k
- **Success Metrics**: Deployment frequency, configuration consistency, onboarding time

### Secondary: Platform Engineering Teams at Scale-ups (100-1000 employees)
**Profile**: Organizations building internal developer platforms
- **Pain Points**: Multi-team coordination, compliance requirements, golden path enforcement
- **Budget Authority**: Platform/Infrastructure VPs with $50-200k budgets
- **Decision Timeline**: 1-3 months with POC requirements
- **Success Metrics**: Developer productivity, compliance adherence, platform adoption

### Tertiary: Individual Contributors & Small Teams (<50 employees)
**Profile**: Senior engineers and consultants managing client environments
- **Pain Points**: Context switching between projects, client-specific configurations
- **Budget Authority**: Personal/team budgets under $1k annually
- **Decision Timeline**: Immediate for individual plans
- **Success Metrics**: Personal productivity, error reduction

## Pricing Model

### Freemium SaaS with Open-Source Core

**Free Tier (Open Source)**
- Core CLI functionality
- Basic configuration management
- Community support
- Single cluster support
- Individual use

**Professional ($29/user/month)**
- Multi-cluster management
- Configuration templates & policies
- Git integration with approval workflows
- Audit logs (30 days)
- Email support
- Up to 10 users

**Enterprise ($99/user/month)**
- Advanced RBAC and SSO integration
- Extended audit logs (1 year)
- Custom policy frameworks
- Priority support with SLA
- Professional services credits
- Unlimited users with volume discounts

**Rationale**: 
- Free tier captures existing community and drives adoption
- Professional tier targets sweet spot for mid-market teams
- Enterprise tier provides growth path with high-value features

## What We Will Explicitly NOT Do Yet

### No Premature Scaling
- **No additional hires** until $50k MRR achieved
- **No office space** - remain fully remote
- **No expensive conferences** beyond 2-3 strategic events
- **No paid advertising** - focus on organic growth

### No Feature Sprawl
- **No CI/CD pipeline features** - integrate, don't compete
- **No monitoring/observability** - partner with existing solutions
- **No infrastructure provisioning** - focus on configuration management
- **No custom Kubernetes operators** - maintain tool simplicity

### No Complex Sales Motion
- **No inside sales team** until $100k MRR
- **No channel partnerships** beyond technical integrations
- **No reseller programs** in year one
- **No complex enterprise customization** - standardize features

### No Geographic Expansion
- **No international entities** - US/English market only
- **No localization** of product or content
- **No timezone-specific support** - async-first approach
- **No compliance certifications** (SOC2, etc.) until enterprise traction
