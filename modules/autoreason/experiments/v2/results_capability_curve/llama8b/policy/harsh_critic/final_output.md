**Revised Policy Memo: AI Tool Usage**

**Purpose and Scope**

[Company Name] is committed to fostering a culture of responsible innovation and technological advancement. This policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for the use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools by employees for work-related purposes, ensuring the secure and compliant use of AI tools while protecting the company's intellectual property, data, and reputation.

**Definitions**

The following definitions are used throughout this policy:

* **AI tool**: Software or services utilizing machine learning, natural language processing, or other AI technologies, as defined by leading industry organizations, such as the International Organization for Standardization (ISO) and the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST).
* **Approved AI tool**: An AI tool that has been specifically approved by the technology coordinator or manager after thorough evaluation, testing, and meeting the necessary criteria.
* **Work-related purposes**: Any activity related to an employee's job responsibilities, including: 
	+ Development, testing, evaluation, research, and training
	+ Development, testing, and training of AI models and algorithms
	+ As defined by job descriptions and performance expectations
* **Data protection**: The protection of personally identifiable information (PII) and sensitive data in accordance with existing Data Protection Agreement (DPA) terms and relevant laws and regulations (e.g., GDPR, DPA).
* **Intellectual property**: The protection of company-owned and licensed intellectual property, including copyrights, trademarks, and trade secrets.
* **Machine learning platform**: Software or services that enable the development and training of machine learning models.
* **Natural language processing tool**: Software or services that enable the processing and analysis of human language.
* **Computer vision software**: Software or services that enable the processing and analysis of visual data.
* **Compliance**: The adherence to relevant laws, regulations, and company policies.
* **Security**: The protection of company data, intellectual property, and IT systems from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, modification, or destruction.
* **Technology coordinator**: The designated employee responsible for overseeing the use of AI tools within the company.
* **Manager**: The supervisor of an employee, responsible for ensuring their team understands and complies with this policy.

**Approved AI Tools and Uses**

The following AI tools have been approved for use by employees for work-related purposes:

1. GitHub Copilot Business (up to 80 seats, already licensed, with specific use cases and guidelines)
2. Company Slack (limited to approved features and settings)

Additional AI tools may be approved by the technology coordinator or manager, subject to thorough evaluation, testing, and adherence to necessary criteria.

**Prohibited Uses**

Employees may not:

1. **Paste customer PII or financial data into third-party AI services**: This may violate existing DPA terms and compromise data protection obligations.
2. **Use AI-generated code with GPL license headers**: AI-generated code may not be copyrightable, and the GPL would apply to the training data, posing intellectual property risks.
3. **Use AI-generated text that includes copyrighted material from competitors**: This may infringe on the competitor's copyright and compromise the company's intellectual property.
4. **Use AI tools to generate code, text, or other materials that compromise the company's SOC2 Type II certification or GDPR obligations**: Such activities may pose significant security and compliance risks.
5. **Use AI tools to store or process sensitive data without proper authorization**: This may compromise data protection and intellectual property obligations.
6. **Use AI tools for personal gain or to benefit competitors**: This may compromise the company's intellectual property and reputation.
7. **Disclose confidential company information to unauthorized parties**: This may compromise the company's intellectual property and reputation.

**Security and Compliance**

1. Employees must use approved AI tools and follow all applicable security protocols to protect company data and intellectual property.
2. Employees must comply with all applicable company policies, including data protection and intellectual property policies.
3. Employees must report any suspected security breaches or compliance issues to the technology coordinator or manager immediately.
4. The company will conduct regular security audits and risk assessments to ensure the secure use of AI tools.
5. Employees must participate in regular security awareness training to ensure they understand the risks and consequences of non-compliance.

**Training and Education**

1. The company will provide regular training and guidance to employees on the use of AI tools and this policy.
2. Employees must complete regular training and certification on AI tool usage and data protection.
3. Managers and technology coordinators will be responsible for ensuring that their teams understand and comply with this policy, and will provide regular feedback and coaching.
4. The company will provide a dedicated training program for employees who will be using AI tools for work-related purposes.

**Acknowledgement**

By using any AI tool, employees acknowledge that they have read, understood, and will comply with this policy and all applicable guidelines and protocols.
