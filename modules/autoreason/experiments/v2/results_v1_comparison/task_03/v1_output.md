# HYBRID WORK POLICY
## Balancing Flexibility with Collaboration

### EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This policy establishes a flexible hybrid framework that respects Engineering's remote-first preference while addressing Sales' collaboration needs. The approach centers on outcomes over presence, with structured collaboration windows and role-specific flexibility.

---

## 1. REQUIRED IN-OFFICE PRESENCE

### Core Framework: "Minimum Viable Presence" Model

**Engineering Team (20 people) - Remote-First Track**
- **Minimum requirement:** 1 day per quarter for team planning sessions
- **Recommended:** Monthly team lunch (optional, company-funded)
- **No banking required:** Quarterly sessions are scheduled in advance

**Sales Team (15 people) - Collaboration Track**  
- **Minimum requirement:** 2 days per week in office (Tuesday-Wednesday core)
- **Client accommodation:** Additional days as needed for client meetings
- **Flexibility:** Other days remote unless client/prospect meetings require office presence

**Leadership & Other Functions (15 people) - Hybrid Track**
- **Minimum requirement:** 1 day per week in office
- **Recommended:** Tuesdays for maximum overlap with sales team
- **Flexibility:** Schedule around team needs and meetings

*FIXES PROBLEM 5 & 7: Simplified requirements eliminate complex tracking and banking systems*

### Office Coordination
- **Core office days:** Tuesday-Wednesday (sales team anchor days)
- **Hot-desk reservation:** 24-hour advance booking required
- **Team spaces:** Dedicated areas for sales team, bookable spaces for engineering

## 2. MEETING NORMS

### Practical Meeting Standards

**Hybrid meeting setup:**
- In-office attendees may share conference rooms with proper AV equipment

**Core Collaboration Window:** 11 AM - 2 PM ET (8 AM - 11 AM PT, 5 PM - 8 PM CET)
- All critical cross-team meetings within this window
- EU team participates during their end-of-day (5-8 PM CET) twice weekly maximum

*FIXES PROBLEM 2: Creates realistic 3-hour window that works across all zones*

- **Engineering standups:** Async-first via Slack, weekly 30-min sync optional

- **EU coordination:** Monday/Thursday during core window only
- **One-on-ones:** Manager discretion, accommodation for all time zones

**Asynchronous Communication:**
- Decision documentation within 48 hours

- 24-hour response expectation during business hours
