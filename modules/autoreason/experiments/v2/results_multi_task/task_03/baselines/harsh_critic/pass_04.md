# HYBRID WORK POLICY: FUNCTION-OPTIMIZED FRAMEWORK
**Effective Date:** [Date + 21 days]  
**Policy Version:** 1.0

## Executive Decision

**This company will optimize work arrangements by function to maximize business results, not to satisfy individual preferences or create artificial equality.**

The CEO's commitment: We will design work patterns that make each function most effective at serving customers and growing the business.

## Core Operating Principle

**Different functions require different collaboration patterns. We optimize for business results, not uniform policies.**

## Function-Specific Work Models

### Engineering Team (20 people): Output-Optimized Remote
**Primary Location:** Remote with strategic in-person collaboration
**Office Requirements:** 
- **Monthly team days:** First Tuesday of each month (mandatory)
- **Quarterly planning:** 2 consecutive days per quarter (mandatory)
- **Project-driven collaboration:** As needed for complex architecture decisions or major releases
- **No routine office requirements**

**Business Logic:** Engineering productivity peaks with uninterrupted focus time. In-person time reserved for high-value collaborative work that benefits from physical presence.

### Sales Team (15 people): Relationship-Optimized Hybrid
**Primary Location:** Office-centric with remote flexibility
**Office Requirements:**
- **Monday-Wednesday-Thursday:** In office (mandatory)
- **Tuesday-Friday:** Remote option for client calls and proposal work
- **Client meeting days:** Match client location/preference

**Business Logic:** Sales success depends on team energy, rapid information sharing, and collaborative problem-solving. Office presence builds relationships and accelerates deal velocity.

### Support/Operations (15 people): Coverage-Optimized Flexible
**Primary Location:** Hybrid with coverage priorities
**Office Requirements:**
- **Two anchor days per week:** Choose Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday (mandatory)
- **Coverage coordination:** Ensure office presence during peak customer hours
- **All-hands participation:** Monthly company meetings (mandatory)

**Business Logic:** Support requires consistent availability and cross-functional coordination while maintaining work-life balance for sustainable customer service.

## Timezone Integration Strategy

### EU Team Integration (3 people)
**Reality:** Full integration is impossible without destroying productivity. We will optimize for strategic alignment and clear decision authority.

**Daily Operations:**
- **EU Authority Hours:** 9 AM - 6 PM CET with full decision-making power within defined scope
- **Critical Overlap:** Tuesday/Wednesday 8:30-9:30 AM EST / 2:30-3:30 PM CET (weekly rotating)
- **Async Priority:** All routine coordination happens via documented handoffs
- **Emergency Protocol:** Direct CEO/department head contact for urgent decisions outside overlap hours

**Monthly Integration:**
- **EU Strategy Session:** 90 minutes monthly with CEO and department heads
- **Quarterly Planning:** EU team lead travels to US for 1 week (company-funded)
- **Project Ownership:** EU team owns specific initiatives with clear success metrics and minimal US dependency

### US Coast Coordination
**West Coast Accommodation:**
- **Core overlap:** 11 AM - 2 PM PST / 2 PM - 5 PM EST daily
- **Meeting rotation:** Alternate weekly between 10 AM EST and 2 PM EST start times
- **Travel requirement:** West Coast team attends quarterly planning in-person

## Meeting and Communication Framework

### Meeting Hierarchy
**Tier 1 - Strategic (Monthly):**
- All-hands company meeting
- Department planning sessions
- Cross-functional project launches
- **Format:** In-person required for office-based teams, video for remote participants

**Tier 2 - Tactical (Weekly):**
- Department standups
- Project status reviews
- Client check-ins
- **Format:** Default video, in-person when majority of participants are office-present

**Tier 3 - Operational (Daily):**
- Quick problem-solving
- Information sharing
- Individual check-ins
- **Format:** Slack-first, escalate to quick video calls when needed

### Communication Response Standards
**Immediate Response (within 2 hours during business hours):**
- Customer escalations
- Revenue-impacting decisions
- Blocking issues for teammates

**Same-Day Response:**
- Project questions and updates
- Meeting requests and scheduling
- Non-urgent client communications

**Next-Business-Day Response:**
- Strategic planning input
- Process improvement ideas
- Professional development requests

**Quarterly OKR Achievement:**
1. **Goal Completion** - Measurable outcomes specific to role and business impact
2. **Quality Standards** - Stakeholder satisfaction and deliverable excellence
3. **Team Contribution** - How your work enabled others' success across functions

### Function-Specific Excellence (40% of review)

- **Code Quality:** Peer review scores and bug rates
- **Architecture Decisions:** Long-term system impact and scalability
- **Knowledge Sharing:** Documentation quality and mentoring contributions

**Sales Assessment:**

- **Client Relationships:** Satisfaction scores and renewal rates

**Support/Operations Assessment:**

- **Process Improvement:** Efficiency gains and system optimization
- **Cross-Team Coordination:** Collaboration effectiveness with engineering and sales

**Weekly Check-ins (10 minutes):**
- Obstacle identification and immediate support needs
- Quick progress pulse on quarterly goals
- **Format:** Video call regardless of location

- Comprehensive goal progress review
- Professional development planning
- Resource needs and team dynamics
- **Format:** In-person when both parties are office-present, otherwise video

**Quarterly Reviews (90 minutes):**
- Complete OKR assessment with evidence and peer feedback
- Compensation and career progression discussion
- Next quarter goal setting and resource planning
- **Format:** Always in-person (travel arranged if necessary)

- **Quarterly bias audits** reviewing performance ratings by work location and function
- **Anonymous feedback system** for reporting unfair treatment
- **Manager training** on remote performance assessment and unconscious bias
