# PRACTICAL HYBRID WORK POLICY
**Effective Date:** [Date + 2 weeks]  
**Policy Version:** 1.0

## Core Principle: Business Results Over Location Theater

This policy optimizes for business outcomes, not work location preferences. Different roles require different environments. We accommodate this reality without creating operational complexity.

## Work Location Requirements

### Engineering Team: Remote-Default, Strategic Office Use
- **Office requirement:** 4 days per month, self-scheduled
- **Purpose:** Architecture planning, onboarding, team building
- **No daily minimums or maximums**

### Sales Team: Office-Majority for Energy and Collaboration  
- **Office requirement:** 3 days per week minimum
- **Recommended pattern:** Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday
- **Purpose:** Pipeline reviews, deal strategy, team motivation

### All Other Roles: Flexible Based on Function
- **Office requirement:** 2 days per week average
- **Coordination:** Schedule around engineering/sales needs
- **Purpose:** Cross-team collaboration, vendor meetings

## EU Team Integration: Practical Approach

**Daily Overlap Window:** 9:00-10:00 AM EST / 3:00-4:00 PM CET
- **Purpose:** Urgent decisions and real-time coordination only
- **Duration:** 1 hour maximum daily
- **Scope:** Customer issues, technical blockers, strategic decisions

**Weekly EU-US Sync:** Thursdays 9:00 AM EST / 3:00 PM CET (30 minutes)
- Week accomplishments and next week priorities
- Blockers requiring US team support
- Cross-team project updates

**Quarterly Integration:** One EU team member visits US office for strategic planning and relationship building.

## Office Infrastructure: Right-Sized and Functional

**Space Configuration:**
- 30 desks total (adequate for peak usage of 25-28 people)
- 2 meeting rooms: 1 small (6 people), 1 large (16 people)  
- 3 phone booths for private calls
- Open collaboration area with whiteboards

**Technology:**
- High-speed internet with backup
- WiFi throughout office
- Video conferencing in both meeting rooms
- Shared monitors at each desk

## Equipment Policy: Performance-Focused, Cost-Effective

**Standard Setup for All Roles:**
- MacBook Pro 14" M3 (32GB RAM, 1TB SSD): $1,800
- Basic accessories (monitor, keyboard, mouse): $200
- **Total per person: $2,000**

**Equipment Management:**
- 3-year replacement cycle
- Employee responsible for loss/damage
- IT vendor handles repairs and warranty
- Backup equipment pool for emergencies

## Meeting and Communication Standards

### Meeting Optimization
- Default meeting length: 25 minutes
- Video required for meetings under 8 people
- EU-inclusive meetings: 9:00-10:00 AM EST only
- No meetings before 9 AM or after 5 PM local time

### Response Time Expectations (Business Hours)
- **Urgent (customer/system issues):** 1 hour
- **Standard (project coordination):** 4 hours  
- **Routine (administrative):** 24 hours

### Documentation Requirements
- Strategic decisions documented within 24 hours
- Weekly written updates from each team
- Meeting recordings available for EU team

## Performance Evaluation: Results-Only Focus

### Primary Evaluation Criteria (100% Results-Based)
- **Goal Achievement:** OKRs, revenue targets, project delivery
- **Quality Metrics:** Customer satisfaction, code quality, process improvement
- **Team Contribution:** Knowledge sharing, cross-functional collaboration
- **Professional Growth:** Skill development, increased responsibility

### Evaluation Process
- Weekly 1:1 meetings (location flexible)
- Monthly goal review and support planning
- Quarterly comprehensive performance review
- Annual compensation and promotion review

### Location Neutrality Guarantee
- Performance evaluation ignores work location
- Promotion criteria based solely on results and impact
- Equal access to growth opportunities regardless of office attendance
- Manager training focused on results-based evaluation (4 hours total)

**Week 1:**
- Office space preparation and basic technology setup
- Equipment ordering and initial distribution
- Team communication about new policy
- Manager briefing on results-focused evaluation
