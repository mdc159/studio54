## Business-Driven Work Allocation

### Revenue-Critical Functions
**Sales Team (15 people) - Office-Centric Model**
- **Business Rationale:** Client relationships and deal collaboration require in-person energy
- **Requirement:** 4 days/week in office (Monday-Thursday)
- **Friday Rule:** Remote for pipeline work, client calls, and planning
- **Override:** Client meetings always take priority over office requirements
- **Accountability:** Revenue targets and client satisfaction scores (not attendance)

### Product Development Functions  
**Engineering Team (20 people) - Remote-Optimized Model**
- **Business Rationale:** Deep work and code quality suffer from office interruptions
- **Requirement:** 1 day/month team synchronization (scheduled by team leads)
- **Focus Protection:** No mandatory meetings Monday/Friday
- **Collaboration Window:** Tuesday-Thursday for cross-team coordination when needed
- **Accountability:** Sprint deliverables and code quality metrics (not presence)

### Support Functions (15 people) - Hybrid Model**
- **Business Rationale:** Mix of deep work and cross-functional collaboration
- **Requirement:** Wednesday (company sync day) + team-determined second day
- **Department Autonomy:** Each function (Marketing, Finance, Operations) chooses its optimal pattern
- **Accountability:** Project delivery and stakeholder satisfaction (not location tracking)

## Timezone Reality Management

### EU Team Integration Strategy
**Acknowledgment:** 3 EU employees cannot be fully integrated into US operations without sacrificing their work-life balance or US team productivity.

**EU Team Autonomy Model:**
- **Local Leadership:** EU team lead has decision-making authority for regional priorities
- **US Integration:** 2-hour daily overlap window (2-4 PM CET / 8-10 AM EST)
- **Asynchronous Handoffs:** All EU→US work documented in shared systems by 4 PM CET
- **Quarterly Alignment:** EU lead visits US for strategic planning (company-funded)

**Communication Protocol:**
- **Same-Day Urgent:** 8-10 AM EST overlap window only
- **Next-Day Standard:** Documented in project management systems
- **Weekly Sync:** EU team lead joins US leadership call (9 AM EST = 3 PM CET)
- **No Timezone Martyrdom:** No 6 AM or 11 PM calls for routine business

### US Coast Coordination
**West Coast Team (assumed 10 people):**
- **Core Overlap:** 10 AM - 3 PM PST / 1 PM - 6 PM EST
- **Meeting Scheduling:** Rotating weekly between coast-friendly times
- **Travel Expectation:** West Coast visits HQ twice per year for strategic sessions
