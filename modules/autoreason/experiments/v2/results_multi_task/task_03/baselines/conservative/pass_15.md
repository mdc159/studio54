# Hybrid Remote Work Policy
**Effective Date:** [Date]  
**Policy Version:** 1.0

## Executive Summary

This policy establishes a flexible hybrid work model that balances Engineering's remote-first preferences with Sales' collaboration needs while maintaining operational efficiency across multiple time zones. The policy emphasizes outcomes over location while providing structured touchpoints for culture building and strategic alignment.

## Core Principles

- **Function-First Flexibility**: Department-specific requirements based on role demands
- **Outcome-Based Performance**: Location independence with clear deliverable expectations  
- **Intentional Collaboration**: Structured in-person time for maximum impact
- **Global Inclusion**: Timezone-conscious practices ensuring EU team participation

## Required In-Office Days

### Engineering Team (20 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 1 day per month in office
- **Recommended**: 4-6 days per quarter
- **Mandatory Dates**: 
  - Quarterly planning sessions (2 days per quarter)
  - Annual company retreat
  - Sprint retrospectives with cross-functional teams (monthly)

### Sales Team (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 3 days per week in office
- **Core Days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Flexible Days**: Monday and Friday (team choice)
- **Exceptions**: Travel for client meetings, approved remote work requests

### All Other Functions (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 2 days per week in office
- **Core Days**: Wednesday (all-hands alignment) + one additional day
- **Department Alignment**: Coordinate with primary collaboration partners

### Special Circumstances
- **EU Team Members (3 people)**: Quarterly US office visits (company-sponsored)
- **New Hires**: First 30 days require 3 days/week in office for onboarding
- **Performance Issues**: May require increased office presence at manager discretion

## Meeting Norms

### Timezone Accommodation
- **Core Overlap Hours**: 11 AM - 2 PM EST (8 AM - 11 AM PST, 4 PM - 7 PM CET)
- **All-Hands Meetings**: Rotating times monthly to share timezone burden
- **EU Inclusion**: Maximum 2 meetings per week outside EU-friendly hours per person

### Meeting Structure
- **Default Mode**: Hybrid-optimized (assume some participants are remote)
- **Technology Standard**: All meetings start with video/audio check
- **Documentation**: Required meeting notes within 24 hours
- **Recording Policy**: All strategic meetings recorded for async review

### In-Person Meeting Priorities
1. Quarterly business reviews and planning
2. Cross-functional project kickoffs
3. Performance reviews and career development discussions
4. Client presentations and deal closings (Sales)
5. Architecture and design reviews (Engineering)

## Equipment and Technology Policy

### Company-Provided Equipment (All Employees)
- Laptop (MacBook Pro or equivalent)
- External monitor (24" minimum)
- Noise-canceling headphones
- Webcam (if laptop camera insufficient)
- Ergonomic keyboard and mouse

### Home Office Stipend
- **Amount**: $1,000 one-time setup allowance
- **Annual Refresh**: $300 per year for equipment updates
- **Eligible Items**: Desk, chair, lighting, internet upgrade, monitor arms

### Office Equipment Access
- **Hot-desking**: First-come, first-served basis with advance booking system
- **Reserved Spaces**: Available for booking up to 2 weeks in advance
- **Permanent Desks**: Sales team members only (due to 3-day requirement)
- **Shared Resources**: Conference rooms, presentation equipment, whiteboards

### IT Support
- **Remote Support**: Available 8 AM - 6 PM EST weekdays
- **On-site Support**: Available during core office hours
- **Equipment Replacement**: 2-day turnaround for critical failures
- **VPN and Security**: Mandatory for all remote connections

## Performance Evaluation Approach

### Core Evaluation Principles
- **Output-Based Metrics**: Focus on deliverables and results, not hours or location
- **Regular Check-ins**: Bi-weekly 1:1s regardless of work location
- **360-Degree Feedback**: Include perspectives from both remote and in-office colleagues

### Department-Specific Metrics

#### Engineering
- Code quality and review participation
- Sprint commitment achievement
- Technical documentation contribution
- Cross-team collaboration effectiveness
- Innovation and problem-solving contributions

#### Sales
- Revenue targets and pipeline management
- Client relationship development
- Team collaboration and knowledge sharing
- Market intelligence gathering
- Mentorship of junior team members

#### All Functions
- Goal achievement and deadline adherence
- Communication effectiveness across work modes
- Team collaboration and culture contribution
- Professional development progress
- Company value demonstration

### Performance Review Process
- **Frequency**: Quarterly informal reviews, bi-annual formal reviews
- **Format**: Video-first for consistency across locations
- **Documentation**: Structured feedback forms with specific examples
- **Career Development**: Individual development plans with location-flexible growth paths

## Timezone Management

### Core Business Hours by Region
- **US East Coast**: 9 AM - 5 PM EST
- **US West Coast**: 9 AM - 5 PM PST  
- **EU Team**: 9 AM - 5 PM CET

### Collaboration Windows
- **Daily Standup Window**: 11 AM - 12 PM EST (maximum 15 minutes)
- **Cross-Regional Projects**: Designated overlap hours for real-time collaboration
- **Emergency Escalation**: 24-hour response protocol with regional handoffs

### Communication Protocols
- **Urgent (< 4 hours)**: Slack with @channel mention
- **Same-day (< 8 hours)**: Direct Slack message
- **Next business day**: Email or regular Slack
- **Non-urgent**: Async documentation in project tools

### EU Team Integration
- **Monthly EU Focus Sessions**: 7-9 AM EST for EU-friendly timing
- **Quarterly Visits**: Company-sponsored US office visits
- **Local Meetups**: Budget allocation for EU team local gatherings
- **Cultural Integration**: EU holidays recognized, flexible PTO for cultural observances

## Policy Governance

### Review Schedule
- **Monthly**: Utilization metrics and feedback review
- **Quarterly**: Policy effectiveness assessment with leadership team
- **Annually**: Comprehensive policy review with all stakeholder input

### Compliance Monitoring
- **Metrics Tracked**: Office utilization, meeting participation, collaboration scores
- **Reporting**: Monthly dashboard to leadership team
- **Escalation**: Department head discussion for consistent non-compliance

### Amendment Process
- **Minor Changes**: Department head approval with HR consultation
- **Major Changes**: Leadership team approval with 30-day advance notice
- **Emergency Adjustments**: CEO approval with retroactive team notification

- Employee satisfaction scores (target: >4.0/5.0)
- Office utilization rates by department
- Meeting participation and engagement scores
- Performance review ratings consistency across work modes
- Retention rates by work location preference

### Qualitative Measures
- Cross-functional collaboration effectiveness
- Cultural cohesion and company value alignment
- Innovation and creative output
- Client satisfaction and relationship quality
- Employee feedback on work-life balance
