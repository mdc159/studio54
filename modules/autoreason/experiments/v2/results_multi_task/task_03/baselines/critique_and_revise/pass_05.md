## Critical Review of the Final Revision

### Real Problems Identified:

1. **Budget Math Completely Wrong**: Claims $86,575 annual cost but task requires "under $65K annually." Equipment allocation of $1,950 per person × 50 people = $97,500 initial cost, not the $24,375 amortized figure used.

2. **Engineering Attendance Requirement Unenforceable**: 6 days per year is so minimal it's meaningless. "Missing >2 days triggers review" means someone could skip 33% of required time with just a performance conversation.

3. **Sales Flexibility Loophole**: 85% quarterly quota allows remote flexibility, but what if entire sales team hits 85%? Then nobody's in office on the "required" collaboration days, defeating the purpose.

4. **Core Hours Math Error**: Claims 10 AM-12 PM EST is global overlap, but that's 7-9 AM PST (brutal for West Coast) and 3-5 PM CET (end of EU workday). Not sustainable daily.

5. **Desk Math Still Wrong**: Claims 35 desks needed but calculates Sales (15) + Others (3.75) + Engineering (2-3) = 21-22. But "Others" are required 4 days/quarter = 1 day/month = 1.25 people/day average, not 3.75.

6. **Equipment Budget Ignores Shipping/Setup**: $97,500 equipment + $30K internet + $15K phones + $7.2K EU + $10K replacement = $159,700, not $86,575. Missing IT support costs entirely.

7. **Performance Metrics Unmeasurable**: "Cross-team project participation" - how measured? "Internal customer satisfaction" - who measures this and how often? "Speaking time distribution" - penalizes introverts.

8. **EU Team Isolation**: No US travel requirement means EU team never meets colleagues in person, creating permanent second-class status despite "virtual-first" language.
