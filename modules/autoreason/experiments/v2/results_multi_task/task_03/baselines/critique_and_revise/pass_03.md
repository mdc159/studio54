## Critical Review of the Final Revision

### Real Problems Identified:

1. **Engineering Quarterly Math Still Broken**: Q1/Q3 have 2-day quarterly planning + Q2/Q4 have 2-day all-hands + retrospective. This creates uneven distribution - some quarters require 2 days, others require 2 days, but the policy claims "4 days per quarter" consistently.

2. **Sales Performance Loophole**: "Top 20% performers can negotiate 1-day remote" creates a moving target - who decides rankings? What happens when someone drops out of top 20%? Creates political dynamics.

3. **Equipment Tier System Is Arbitrary**: Why do "hybrid workers" get less equipment when they need portable setups more than full-remote workers? The logic is backwards.

4. **EU Regional Hub Costs Underestimated**: "Quarterly 2-day London meetup" = 4 trips × 3 people × (flight $800 + hotel $300 × 2 nights + meals $200) = $16,800 per quarter = $67K annually, not $18K.

5. **Core Hours Window Too Narrow**: 2 hours daily (10 AM-12 PM EST) for all company-wide business is unrealistic for a 50-person startup with cross-functional dependencies.

6. **Hot Desk Capacity Mismatch**: 25 desks for "up to 35 potential daily users" but Sales alone (12 dedicated + 3 shared = 15 people) + other functions (15 people on Wednesdays) = 30 people minimum on Wednesdays, leaving no buffer.

7. **Calendar Tracking Unenforceable**: People can accept calendar invites and not show up. "Brief attendance confirmation" adds administrative burden without solving the core tracking problem.

# Hybrid Remote Work Policy (FINAL REVISION)

## Executive Summary

This policy establishes a sustainable hybrid model balancing Engineering's remote preference with Sales' collaboration needs while controlling costs under $60K annually. The policy uses business-driven attendance requirements with clear measurement and enforcement mechanisms.

## Required In-Office Presence

### Engineering Team (20 people)
**Minimum Requirement**: 6 days per calendar year
- **Company All-Hands**: 2 days annually (typically Q2 and Q4)
- **Engineering Planning**: 2 days annually (Q1 and Q3) 
- **Team Building**: 1 day annually (combined with all-hands when possible)
- **Individual Choice**: 1 flexible day annually
- **Scheduling**: All mandatory dates published January 1st, no changes without 60-day notice
- **Make-up Policy**: Missed mandatory days must be completed within same quarter

### Sales Team (15 people)
**Minimum Requirement**: 8 days per month (2 days per week average)
- **Core Days**: Tuesdays and Wednesdays (pipeline reviews + cross-team collaboration)
- **Flexibility**: 1 remote day per month for quota-achieving reps (>90% of monthly target)
- **Client Exception**: External client meetings count as office days regardless of location
- **Underperformance**: Below 70% quarterly quota = 3 days/week minimum next quarter

### Other Functions (15 people)
**Minimum Requirement**: 4 days per month (1 day per week average)
- **Company Wednesday**: Monthly company-wide collaboration day (first Wednesday)
- **Department Days**: Department heads set 3 additional days per month
- **Project Surge**: Cross-functional project launches may require additional 2-3 consecutive days with 2-week notice
- **Flexibility**: High performers can bank unused days (max 2 days) for following month

### EU Team Members (3 people)
**Virtual-First Integration**:
- **No US Travel Required** (eliminates $67K annual cost)
- **Virtual Participation**: Full access to all company events via dedicated EU timezone sessions
- **Regional Connection**: Annual 1-day meetup in Amsterdam (company covers travel + accommodation = $3K total)
- **Local Workspace**: $75/month co-working allowance or home office improvement
