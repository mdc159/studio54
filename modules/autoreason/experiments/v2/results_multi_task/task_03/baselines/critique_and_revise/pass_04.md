## Critical Review of the Final Revision

### Real Problems Identified:

1. **Engineering Math Still Wrong**: Policy claims "6 days per calendar year" but lists 5 specific requirements (2+2+1+0=5). The "Individual Choice" day is vague and unenforceable.

2. **Sales Quota Loophole Creates Chaos**: "90% monthly target for flexibility" means someone could hit 90% in January, take remote days in February while performing at 50%, then hit 90% in March. Creates monthly gaming of the system.

3. **EU Cost Calculation Error**: "Annual 1-day Amsterdam meetup = $3K" assumes local travel only. EU team still needs to coordinate with US operations, creating hidden collaboration costs.

4. **Core Hours Window Still Broken**: 9 AM-1 PM EST = 4 hours, but then claims meetings rotate between 9-10 AM and 12-1 PM slots. That's only 2 hours of actual overlap time for company-wide business.

5. **Desk Booking System Unrealistic**: 30 desks for Sales (15 people × 2 days/week = 30 desk-days) + Others (15 people × 1 day/week = 15 desk-days) + Engineering surge days = 45+ desk-days needed but only 30 desks available.

6. **Performance Metrics Are Unmeasurable**: "Story points delivered per sprint vs team average" - what if the whole team is underperforming? "Stakeholder satisfaction surveys" - who are stakeholders and how often are surveys conducted?

7. **Progressive Enforcement Lacks Teeth**: "Role may be converted to office-required position" - what if Engineering refuses? Mass resignations? No realistic consequences for a remote-first team.

8. **Equipment Budget Doesn't Include Shipping/Setup**: $75K for equipment + $24K internet + $2.7K EU = $101.7K, not $57.2K. Missing IT support, shipping, replacement costs.

## Executive Summary

This policy establishes a sustainable hybrid model balancing Engineering's remote preference with Sales' collaboration needs while maintaining operational costs under $65K annually. The policy uses quarterly attendance requirements with clear business justification and measurable enforcement.

## Required In-Office Presence

### Engineering Team (20 people)
**Minimum Requirement**: 6 days per calendar year
- **Company All-Hands**: 2 days annually (Q2 and Q4, typically Tuesday-Wednesday)
- **Engineering Quarterly Planning**: 4 days annually (1 day per quarter, scheduled with all-hands when possible)
- **Scheduling**: All dates published by December 15th for following year
- **Make-up Policy**: Missed days must be completed within 30 days, virtual participation allowed for documented emergencies
- **Enforcement**: Missing >2 days annually triggers individual performance review

### Sales Team (15 people)
**Minimum Requirement**: 32 days per year (8 days per quarter average)
- **Core Collaboration Days**: Tuesdays and Wednesdays each week
- **Quarterly Flexibility**: Reps achieving >85% quarterly quota can work remotely up to 4 Tuesdays OR Wednesdays in following quarter (not both days in same week)
- **Client Meeting Credit**: External client meetings count toward office requirement regardless of location
- **Underperformance Escalation**: <70% quarterly quota = daily office requirement for following quarter

### Other Functions (15 people)
**Minimum Requirement**: 16 days per year (4 days per quarter average)
- **Monthly All-Company Day**: First Wednesday of each month (12 days annually)
- **Department Coordination**: 4 additional days per year scheduled by department heads
- **Project Surge Protocol**: Major launches may require 2-3 consecutive days with 14-day advance notice
- **Banking System**: Unused quarterly days cannot be carried forward

### EU Team Members (3 people)
**Virtual-First with Regional Integration**:
- **No US Travel Required**: Full virtual participation in all company events
- **EU Core Hours**: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM CET (2:00-8:00 AM EST) for EU team coordination
- **Regional Meetup**: Semi-annual 1-day gathering in Amsterdam or London
