# Hybrid Remote Work Policy - Revised Version 4.0
**Effective Date:** [Date]  
**Policy Version:** 4.0

## Executive Summary

This policy establishes a department-specific hybrid work model with enforceable requirements and realistic implementation. Engineering has meaningful in-office commitments tied to actual development cycles, while Sales has structured presence with verified business travel exceptions. The policy includes specific enforcement mechanisms and measurable compliance standards.

## Core Principles

- **Business-Outcome Focused**: Office presence tied to measurable business results
- **Enforceable Implementation**: Clear verification and consequences for non-compliance
- **Consistent Standards**: Same performance expectations regardless of location
- **Operational Realism**: Accommodates actual business workflow patterns and resource constraints

## Required In-Office Days

### Engineering Team (20 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 1 day per week (52 days annually)
- **Scheduling**: Every other Wednesday (26 days) + 26 additional days scheduled monthly based on sprint cycles
- **Purpose**: Sprint planning, architecture reviews, code reviews requiring pair programming, and knowledge transfer
- **Compliance Standard**: Missing more than 8 days annually (15% absence rate) triggers performance improvement plan
- **Enforcement**: Badge-in tracking with 6-hour minimum presence requirement

*Fixes: "Engineering requirements are still meaningless" and "Quarterly planning sessions assume wrong work patterns" - Increases to 52 meaningful days aligned with actual sprint cycles rather than artificial quarterly sessions.*

### Sales Team (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 2 days per week in office when not on verified client business
- **Core Days**: Tuesday and Wednesday (when in town)
- **Client Travel Verification**: Requires client name, meeting purpose, and client contact information submitted 48 hours in advance. Random verification through client contact for 20% of claimed travel.
- **Compliance Standard**: Missing more than 8 core office days annually without verified client business triggers performance improvement plan
- **Enforcement**: Badge-in tracking with 6-hour minimum presence requirement

*Fixes: "Sales travel exception creates massive loophole" - Adds verification mechanism with random client contact to prevent abuse.*

### All Other Functions (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 1 day per week in office
- **Scheduling**: Any day Monday-Friday based on department coordination needs
- **Compliance Standard**: Missing more than 8 days annually triggers performance improvement plan
- **Enforcement**: Badge-in tracking with 6-hour minimum presence requirement

### EU Team Members (3 people)
- **Engineering EU (2 people)**: 2 days per quarter in-person collaboration (travel to US office or designated European hub)
- **Other Functions EU (1 person)**: Available during 8 AM - 10 AM EST overlap window (2 PM - 4 PM CET) for coordination
- **Travel Support**: Company covers quarterly collaboration travel costs up to $2,000 per trip

*Fixes: "Core collaboration hours still exclude EU team" - Shifts to 8 AM - 10 AM EST (2 PM - 4 PM CET) which is mid-afternoon European time, not end-of-day.*

## Meeting Norms

### Meeting Standards
- **Default**: All meetings accommodate both in-person and remote participants equally
- **Video-Required Meetings**: Must be scheduled in equipped conference rooms with 48-hour advance notice
- **Backup Protocol**: If video rooms unavailable, meeting moves to all-remote format
- **Documentation**: Meeting notes with decisions and action items required within 24 hours

### Timezone Management
- **Core Collaboration Hours**: 8 AM - 10 AM EST (5 AM - 7 AM PST, 2 PM - 4 PM CET)
- **Response Expectations**: 
  - Urgent: 4 hours during local business hours
  - Standard: 24 hours
- **EU Integration**: Available during core hours for active project coordination

## Equipment and Technology Policy

### Standard Equipment (All Employees)
- Laptop ($1,500 budget including setup and shipping)
- Docking station ($200 budget)
- External monitor ($400 budget)
- Headset ($150 budget)
- **Total**: $2,250 equipment budget per employee

*Fixes: "Equipment budget ignores procurement reality" - Increases budget to account for shipping, setup, and current market prices.*

### Office Resources
- **Desk Policy**: Reserved desk assignments for high-frequency users
  - Sales team (15 people): Dedicated desks for Tuesday/Wednesday
  - Engineering/Other (35 people): 15 shared desks with mandatory reservation system
  - **Total**: 30 desks for 50 employees
- **Reservation System**: Mandatory 24-hour advance booking through IT ticketing system
  - Violations result in loss of desk reservation privileges for 30 days
  - No walk-in desk usage - unreserved presence results in being sent home

*Fixes: "Shared desk math doesn't work" and "Simple online calendar for desk reservations will break" - Provides dedicated desks for highest-frequency users and mandatory reservation system with enforcement.*

### Meeting Room Infrastructure
- **Video-Equipped Rooms**: 4 rooms with professional video conferencing setup
- **Standard Rooms**: 2 rooms for in-person only meetings
- **Booking**: Mandatory 48-hour advance booking for video rooms, 24-hour for standard rooms
- **Equipment Budget**: $20,000 per video room ($80,000 total)

*Fixes: "Video equipment in only 2 rooms creates bottleneck" - Increases to 4 video rooms with realistic per-room budget.*

## Performance Evaluation Approach

### Universal Standards (All Functions)
- **Attendance Compliance**: Badge tracking data showing required days met (target: 85% annually)
- **Goal Achievement**: Measurable objectives met by deadline (0-100% scale)
- **Communication Responsiveness**: Measured response time compliance during core hours
- **Work Quality**: Role-specific deliverables and deadlines (not collaboration-dependent)

*Fixes: "Performance evaluation metrics are unmeasurable" - Uses badge data for attendance and removes unmeasurable collaboration metrics.*

### Attendance Tracking System
- **Badge System**: Electronic entry tracking with 6-hour minimum presence
- **Weekly Reports**: Automated attendance reports to managers and HR
- **Monthly Review**: Department heads review compliance rates and patterns
- **Violation Process**: 
  - 8+ missed days annually: Mandatory performance improvement plan
  - 15+ missed days annually: Formal disciplinary action up to termination

*Fixes: "Manager observation of attendance patterns is subjective" and "Missing enforcement mechanisms" - Provides objective badge tracking with specific violation thresholds and consequences.*

### Equipment Replacement Policy
- **Standard Replacement**: Every 3 years or upon failure
- **Damage Definition**: Physical damage beyond normal wear, liquid damage, theft
- **Upgrade Policy**: No elective upgrades before 3-year lifecycle
- **Replacement Rate**: 25% annually (accounting for damage, theft, and scheduled refresh)

*Fixes: "Equipment replacement rate is arbitrary" - Provides specific replacement criteria and reduces rate based on 3-year lifecycle.*

## Compliance Tracking

### Attendance Verification
- **Badge System**: Electronic tracking of entry/exit times
- **Minimum Presence**: 6 hours per required day (tracked automatically)
- **Exception Process**: Medical leave and family emergency exceptions require documentation
- **Enforcement Authority**: HR department with department head consultation

*Fixes: "Missing enforcement mechanisms" - Specifies HR as enforcement authority with clear documentation requirements.*

### Reporting
- **Weekly**: Automated compliance reports to managers
- **Monthly**: Department heads review with HR for pattern identification
- **Quarterly**: Individual performance reviews including attendance compliance
