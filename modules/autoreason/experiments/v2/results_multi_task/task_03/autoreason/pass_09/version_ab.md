# Hybrid Remote Work Policy - Synthesized Version
**Effective Date:** [Date]  
**Policy Version:** Synthesized Final

## Executive Summary

This policy establishes a department-specific hybrid work model that balances meaningful in-office collaboration with operational flexibility. Engineering has structured requirements tied to development cycles, Sales has predictable presence with streamlined processes, and all teams operate under measurable performance standards focused on business outcomes.

## Core Principles

- **Business-Outcome Focused**: Office presence tied to measurable business results
- **Practical Implementation**: Realistic expectations for scheduling and compliance  
- **Consistent Standards**: Same performance expectations regardless of location
- **Operational Flexibility**: Accommodates actual business workflow patterns

## Required In-Office Days

### Engineering Team (20 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 2 full-day planning sessions per quarter (8 total days annually)
- **Scheduling**: Quarterly planning dates set at beginning of each quarter with 30-day notice
- **Verification**: Badge scan entry/exit with minimum 6-hour presence
- **Purpose**: Architecture decisions, sprint planning, code reviews requiring pair programming, and knowledge transfer
- **Compliance Standard**: Missing 3+ sessions annually triggers performance review discussion; missing 5+ sessions requires formal improvement plan

*Uses Y's respectful frequency with X's objective verification to eliminate enforcement ambiguity.*

### Sales Team (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 2 days per week in office when not traveling for client business
- **Core Days**: Tuesday and Wednesday (when in town)
- **Verification**: Badge scan entry/exit with minimum 6-hour presence
- **Travel Exception**: Submit weekly travel schedule showing client meetings - no advance approval required for legitimate client business
- **Compliance Standard**: Missing more than 8 core office days annually without documented client business triggers performance improvement plan

*Combines Y's streamlined travel process with X's objective verification and realistic compliance threshold.*

### All Other Functions (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 1 day per week in office
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Any day Monday-Friday based on business needs
- **Verification**: Badge scan entry/exit with minimum 6-hour presence
- **Purpose**: Available for cross-department coordination as needed

### EU Team Members (3 people)
- **Engineering EU (2 people)**: Participate in quarterly planning via video; no required US travel
- **Other Functions EU (1 person)**: Weekly office requirement waived; available during 10 AM - 12 PM EST overlap window for coordination

*Uses Y's approach to avoid legal complications while maintaining coordination.*

## Meeting Norms

### Meeting Standards
- **Default**: All meetings accommodate both in-person and remote participants equally
- **Video-Required Meetings**: Must be scheduled in equipped conference rooms with 48-hour advance notice
- **Backup Protocol**: If video rooms unavailable, meeting moves to all-remote format
- **Documentation**: Meeting notes with decisions and action items required within 24 hours

*Uses X's structured approach with Y's reasonable booking window.*

- **Response Expectations**:

- **EU Integration**: Available during core hours for active project coordination

*Uses Y's corrected timezone window that respects European work-life balance.*
