# Hybrid Remote Work Policy - Executive Synthesis
**Effective Date:** [Date]  
**Policy Version:** Final

## Executive Summary

This policy establishes a workflow-aligned hybrid model that balances meaningful collaboration with operational flexibility. Engineering operates on sprint-based requirements that respect development cycles, Sales maintains predictable presence with streamlined client travel, and all teams operate under objective performance standards focused on measurable business outcomes.

## Core Principles

- **Workflow-Aligned**: Office requirements match actual team collaboration patterns
- **Business-Outcome Focused**: Performance measured by results, not attendance compliance
- **Operationally Realistic**: Infrastructure and processes designed for actual utilization patterns
- **Objectively Measured**: Clear, verifiable standards with structured escalation paths

## Required In-Office Days

### Engineering Team (20 people)
- **Sprint-Aligned Requirement**: 1 day per 2-week sprint for collaborative work
- **Scheduling**: Sprint planning day (typically Monday or Tuesday) determined by Scrum Master with team input
- **Purpose**: Sprint planning, architecture discussions requiring whiteboarding, pair programming sessions, and retrospectives
- **Compliance Standard**: Missing 4+ sprint days in a quarter triggers structured conversation about role fit and support needs
- **Verification**: Scrum Master confirms attendance and collaborative work completion

*Uses Version Y's sprint-based approach - aligns with actual agile workflows rather than artificial quarterly planning, respecting engineering's resistance to arbitrary requirements.*

### Sales Team (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 2 days per week in office when not traveling for client business
- **Core Days**: Tuesday and Wednesday (when in town)
- **Travel Exception**: No advance approval required for client meetings requiring travel - submit travel schedule weekly showing client meetings and expected return
- **Travel Verification**: Client meetings must appear in CRM with expected close date and deal value within 48 hours of return
- **Compliance Standard**: Missing more than 6 core office days per quarter without documented client business triggers performance review

*Combines Version X's streamlined travel process with Version Y's CRM verification - eliminates approval bottlenecks while preventing abuse through post-travel documentation.*

### All Other Functions (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 1 day per week in office
- **Coordination Days**: Wednesday or Thursday to align with Sales presence
- **Purpose**: Cross-department meetings and project coordination
- **Scheduling**: Set monthly based on project needs and office capacity

### EU Team Members (3 people)
- **Engineering EU (2 people)**: Participate in sprint planning via video; no required US travel
- **Other Functions EU (1 person)**: Core hours availability 3-5 PM CET (9-11 AM EST) for coordination; no US travel required

*Uses Version Y's approach - eliminates labor law risks while maintaining reasonable coordination expectations within normal European working hours.*

## Meeting Infrastructure and Norms

### Hybrid Meeting Standards
- **Default Policy**: Meetings are either fully in-person OR fully remote - no hybrid meetings
- **In-Person Meetings**: All participants must be physically present; scheduled only when 100% attendance confirmed
- **Remote Meetings**: All participants join via video regardless of location
- **Emergency Protocol**: If in-person attendee can't attend, meeting automatically becomes all-remote
- **Documentation**: Meeting notes with decisions and action items required within 24 hours

*Uses Version Y's approach to eliminate systematic hybrid meeting failures, with Version X's documentation requirement for accountability.*

### Timezone Management
- **Core Collaboration Hours**: 11 AM - 1 PM EST (8 AM - 10 AM PST, 5 PM - 7 PM CET)
- **Response Expectations**: 
  - Urgent: 4 hours during local business hours
  - Standard: 24 hours
- **EU Integration**: Available during core hours for active project coordination

*Uses Version Y's corrected timezone window that avoids European dinner time disruption.*

- **Attendance Correlation**: Monthly analysis of attendance patterns versus business outcomes

**Attendance Concerns:**
- **Threshold**: Missing 25% of required office days in any quarter
- **Initial Response**: Structured conversation focused on barriers and support needs
- **Documentation**: Written summary of support offered and improvement timeline
- **Escalation**: Formal improvement plan if no progress after 30 days with support
- **Appeals Process**: Employee can request review by department head and HR within 10 business days

*Uses Version Y's supportive, structured approach with clear thresholds and appeals process for legal compliance.*
