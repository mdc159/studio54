# Hybrid Remote Work Policy - Executive Synthesis
**Effective Date:** [Date]  
**Policy Version:** 3.0

## Executive Summary

This policy establishes a department-specific hybrid work model based on demonstrated collaboration needs. Engineering maintains maximum flexibility with minimal office requirements, while Sales has structured in-office time for client-facing activities. The policy prioritizes simple implementation with robust measurement systems.

## Core Principles

- **Evidence-Based Requirements**: Office presence tied to specific business needs
- **Simple Implementation**: Clear rules with straightforward enforcement
- **Consistent Standards**: Same performance expectations regardless of location
- **Practical Operations**: Realistic expectations for scheduling and compliance

## Required In-Office Days

### Engineering Team (20 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 1 day per quarter for team planning session
- **Scheduling**: Team planning date set quarterly, 4 weeks in advance
- **Purpose**: Architecture decisions, sprint planning, and knowledge transfer requiring whiteboarding
- **Enforcement**: Manager tracks attendance; missing 2+ sessions per year triggers performance discussion

*Takes Y's quarterly approach (better for remote-first) with X's enforcement mechanism*

### Sales Team (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 2 days per week in office
- **Core Days**: Tuesday and Wednesday
- **Purpose**: Client calls requiring multiple participants, deal strategy sessions, pipeline reviews
- **Remote Work Process**: Submit request 48 hours in advance with client meeting documentation or emergency details; auto-approved for documented client travel

*Takes Y's 2-day requirement (more realistic) with X's specific approval process*

### All Other Functions (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 1 day per week in office
- **Core Day**: Thursday for cross-team meetings
- **Purpose**: Coordination meetings requiring multiple departments

*Takes X's Thursday timing (avoids conflicts) with Y's simplified approach*

### EU Team Members (3 people)
- **Engineering EU**: Same quarterly sessions via virtual participation at 9 AM EST (3 PM CET)
- **Other Functions EU**: Weekly Thursday sessions via virtual participation at 9 AM EST
- **Annual Visit**: Optional company-sponsored visit, not required

*Takes X's practical timing with Y's virtual participation equality*

## Meeting Norms

### Hybrid Meeting Standards
- **Default**: All meetings are remote-first via video platform
- **In-Person Meetings**: Only for activities requiring physical presence (whiteboarding, equipment setup, client entertainment)
- **Documentation**: Meeting notes required within 48 hours for all decisions involving >3 participants

*Takes Y's simplified approach with X's practical timeframe and scope*

### Timezone Management
- **Core Collaboration Hours**: 11 AM - 1 PM EST (8 AM - 10 AM PST, 5 PM - 7 PM CET)
- **Response Expectations**: 
  - Urgent (marked as such): 4 hours during local business hours
  - Standard: Next business day
- **EU Integration**: Monthly EU-US sync during core hours

*Takes Y's realistic core hours with X's practical response expectations*
