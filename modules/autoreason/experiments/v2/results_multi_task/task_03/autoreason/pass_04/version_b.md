# Hybrid Remote Work Policy - Final Version
**Effective Date:** [Date]  
**Policy Version:** 2.0

## Executive Summary

This policy establishes a department-specific hybrid work model based on demonstrated collaboration needs. Engineering maintains maximum flexibility with minimal office requirements, while Sales has structured in-office time for client-facing activities. The policy prioritizes simple implementation and clear enforcement.

## Core Principles

- **Evidence-Based Requirements**: Office presence tied to specific business needs
- **Simple Implementation**: Clear rules with straightforward enforcement
- **Consistent Standards**: Same performance expectations regardless of location
- **Practical Operations**: Realistic expectations for scheduling and compliance

## Required In-Office Days

### Engineering Team (20 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 2 days per month for team collaboration sessions
- **Scheduling**: Monthly sessions scheduled 6 weeks in advance on first Tuesday and third Wednesday of each month
- **Purpose**: Architecture decisions, sprint planning, and knowledge transfer requiring whiteboarding
- **Enforcement**: Manager tracks attendance; missing 2+ sessions per quarter triggers performance discussion

*Fixes Problem #1: Changes from quarterly to monthly with specific dates and enforcement mechanism*

### Sales Team (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 2 days per week in office
- **Core Days**: Tuesday and Wednesday
- **Purpose**: Client calls requiring multiple participants, deal strategy sessions, pipeline reviews
- **Remote Work Process**: Submit request 48 hours in advance with client meeting documentation or emergency details; auto-approved for documented client travel, manager review required for other requests

*Fixes Problem #5: Defines specific approval criteria and timeframes*

### All Other Functions (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 1 day per week in office
- **Core Day**: Thursday for cross-team meetings
- **Purpose**: Coordination meetings requiring multiple departments

*Addresses scheduling conflicts by moving from Wednesday to Thursday*

### EU Team Members (3 people)
- **Engineering EU**: Same monthly sessions via 9 AM EST start time (3 PM CET)
- **Other Functions EU**: Bi-weekly Thursday sessions (alternating weeks) at 9 AM EST
- **Annual Visit**: Optional company-sponsored visit, not required

*Fixes Problem #3: Changes timing to workable EU hours and reduces frequency for non-engineering*

## Meeting Norms

### Hybrid Meeting Standards
- **Default**: All meetings are remote-first via video platform
- **In-Person Meetings**: Only for activities requiring physical presence (whiteboarding, equipment setup, client entertainment)
- **Documentation**: Decision summary email required within 48 hours for meetings with >3 participants, sent by meeting organizer to all attendees

*Fixes Problem #9: Specifies who documents, what gets documented, and extends timeframe*

### Timezone Management
- **Core Collaboration Hours**: 9 AM - 11 AM EST (6 AM - 8 AM PST, 3 PM - 5 PM CET) Tuesday-Thursday
- **Response Expectations**: 
  - Urgent (marked as such): 4 hours during local business hours
  - Standard: Next business day
- **EU Integration**: Monthly EU-US sync during core hours

*Fixes Problem #4: Extends window, earlier start time, limits to 3 days, accounts for EU working hours*

## Equipment and Technology Policy

### Standard Equipment (All Employees)
- Laptop with docking station
- External monitor
- Headset
- **US employees**: $500 home office setup allowance
- **EU employees**: €450 home office setup allowance (includes VAT and shipping considerations)

*Fixes Problem #7: Provides specific regional amounts accounting for currency and costs*

### Office Resources
- **Desk Capacity**: 40 hot desks available (sufficient for peak Tuesday/Wednesday demand of 30 desks)
- **Desk Booking**: Recurring weekly bookings available for Sales team core days; first-come-first-served daily booking for others
- **Meeting Rooms**: 6 rooms with video conferencing, bookable with desk reservations
- **Equipment Backup**: 10% spare equipment inventory for failures and repairs

*Fixes Problem #2 and #10: Specifies desk capacity, priority system, and integrates meeting room booking*

## Performance Evaluation Approach

### Universal Standards (All Functions)
- Goal achievement and deadline adherence
- **Communication Standards**: Response within defined timeframes (tracked via email/Slack timestamps)
- Professional development progress
- **Office Presence**: Tracked via badge entry/exit logs with 4-hour minimum for compliance

*Fixes Problem #6 and #8: Defines measurable communication standards and uses badge tracking for compliance*

### Performance Issue Process
**Office Presence Issues:**
- Missing required office days triggers automatic manager notification
- 2+ missed sessions per quarter requires formal performance discussion with documented improvement plan
- Resolution through schedule adjustment or role modification

### Measurable Outcomes
- **Office Utilization**: Desk occupancy rate during required days (target: 75-85% of available desks)
- **Response Time Compliance**: Percentage of messages meeting defined response times (target: >90%)
- **Office Presence Compliance**: Percentage of required days attended (target: >95%)
- **Employee Satisfaction**: Quarterly survey with specific hybrid work questions (baseline to be established in Month 1)

*Fixes Problem #11: Provides specific, measurable metrics with realistic targets and baseline establishment*
