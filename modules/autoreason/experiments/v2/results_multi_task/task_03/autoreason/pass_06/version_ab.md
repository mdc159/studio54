# Hybrid Remote Work Policy - Final Version
**Effective Date:** [Date]  
**Policy Version:** Final

## Executive Summary

This policy establishes a department-specific hybrid work model based on demonstrated collaboration needs. Engineering maintains maximum flexibility with minimal office requirements, while Sales has structured in-office time for client-facing activities. The policy prioritizes measurable business outcomes with practical implementation standards.

## Core Principles

- **Business-Outcome Focused**: Office presence tied to measurable business results
- **Measurable Implementation**: Clear metrics with realistic enforcement standards  
- **Consistent Standards**: Same performance expectations regardless of location
- **Practical Operations**: Accommodates actual business workflow patterns

## Required In-Office Days

### Engineering Team (20 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 2 full-day planning sessions per quarter (8 total days annually)
- **Scheduling**: Quarterly planning dates set at beginning of each quarter with 30-day notice
- **Duration**: Full business day (6+ hours of collaborative work)
- **Purpose**: Architecture decisions, sprint planning, and knowledge transfer requiring physical collaboration
- **Compliance Standard**: Missing 3+ sessions annually triggers performance review discussion; missing 5+ sessions requires formal improvement plan

*Synthesis: Takes Version X's meaningful 8-day requirement with escalating consequences, but maintains Version Y's business-day duration clarity.*

### Sales Team (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 2 days per week in office when not traveling for client business
- **Core Days**: Tuesday and Wednesday (when in town)
- **Duration**: Full business day (6+ hours of office presence, with 1-hour lunch flexibility)
- **Travel Exception**: No advance approval required for client meetings requiring travel - submit travel schedule weekly showing client meetings and expected return
- **Other Remote Work**: Submit written request 48 hours in advance with business justification. Manager must respond within 24 hours with written approval/denial.

*Synthesis: Combines Version X's elimination of client travel bottlenecks with Version Y's structured approval process for non-travel exceptions.*

### All Other Functions (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 1 day per week in office
- **Core Day**: Thursday (for cross-department coordination)
- **Duration**: Full business day (6+ hours of office presence, with 1-hour lunch flexibility)
- **Purpose**: Available for cross-department coordination as needed

*Synthesis: Takes Version Y's Thursday assignment to avoid desk conflicts while maintaining Version X's business needs focus.*

### EU Team Members (3 people)
- **Engineering EU (2 people)**: Participate in quarterly planning via video; no required US travel
- **Other Functions EU (1 person)**: Weekly office requirement waived; available during 10 AM - 12 PM EST overlap window for coordination

*Synthesis: Version X's approach eliminates legal and practical issues with mandatory travel while maintaining necessary coordination.*

## Meeting Norms

### Meeting Standards
- **Default**: All meetings accommodate both in-person and remote participants equally
- **In-Person Preferred**: Physical whiteboarding sessions, equipment demonstrations, sensitive client discussions
- **Documentation**: Meeting notes with decisions and action items required within 24 hours

### Timezone Management
- **Core Collaboration Hours**: 10 AM - 12 PM EST (7 AM - 9 AM PST, 4 PM - 6 PM CET)
- **Response Expectations**: 
  - Urgent: 4 hours during local business hours
  - Standard: 24 hours
- **EU Integration**: Available during core hours for active project coordination

*Synthesis: Version X's earlier time window avoids European dinner conflicts while maintaining Version Y's clear documentation requirements.*

## Performance Evaluation Approach

### Universal Standards (All Functions)
- **Goal Achievement**: Measurable objectives met by deadline (0-100% scale)
- **Communication Effectiveness**: Response time compliance (measured quarterly)
- **Office Presence Compliance**: Attendance tracking for pattern identification and business impact assessment
- **Collaboration Quality**: Project completion rates and stakeholder satisfaction for collaborative work

### Performance Issue Process
**Attendance Pattern Concerns:**
- **Engineering**: Missing 3+ annual sessions triggers conversation; 5+ requires formal improvement plan
- **Sales/Others**: Missing 3+ required days per quarter triggers conversation; 6+ requires improvement plan  
- **Resolution**: Collaborative problem-solving focused on business impact and work arrangement effectiveness
- **Documentation**: Written improvement plan with specific targets and 60-day review period

*Synthesis: Combines Version Y's specific thresholds with Version X's business-impact focus and collaborative resolution approach.*

## Compliance Tracking

### Attendance Verification
- **Badge System**: Electronic entry/exit tracking for all office visits
- **Manager Verification**: Weekly attendance reports showing badge times for required days
- **Minimum Presence**: Badge entry before 10 AM, exit after 4 PM on required days (6+ hour minimum)
- **Business Impact Focus**: Track correlation between attendance patterns and team/individual performance outcomes

*Synthesis: Version Y's measurable badge system with Version X's business impact correlation analysis.*

### Reporting
- **Weekly**: Manager review of attendance reports
- **Monthly**: Department heads review attendance patterns and business impact
- **Quarterly**: Individual performance review including work arrangement effectiveness
