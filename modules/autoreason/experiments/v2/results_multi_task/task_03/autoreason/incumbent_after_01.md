# Hybrid Remote Work Policy
**Effective Date:** [Date]  
**Policy Version:** 1.0

## Executive Summary

This policy establishes a function-specific hybrid work model that balances Engineering's remote-first preferences with Sales' collaboration needs while maintaining operational efficiency across multiple time zones. The policy emphasizes practical implementation with clear enforcement mechanisms and realistic expectations.

## Core Principles

- **Function-First Flexibility**: Department-specific requirements based on actual role demands
- **Outcome-Based Performance**: Location independence with clear deliverable expectations  
- **Practical Timezone Management**: Realistic expectations across regions
- **Sustainable Operations**: Policies designed for long-term maintainability

## Required In-Office Days

### Engineering Team (20 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 4 days per quarter (1 day per month average)
- **Mandatory Events**: 
  - Quarterly planning sessions (1 day per quarter)
  - Annual company retreat
- **Flexible Days**: Additional days for specific project needs
- **EU Engineers**: Virtual participation only; no mandatory US travel

### Sales Team (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 3 days per week in office
- **Core Days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Flexible Days**: Monday and Friday (optional)
- **Exceptions**: Client travel, pre-approved remote work requests

### All Other Functions (15 people)
- **Minimum Requirement**: 1 day per week in office
- **Recommended Day**: Wednesday for cross-team alignment
- **Flexibility**: Choose any day based on collaboration needs

### Special Circumstances
- **New Hires**: First 30 days require 4 days/week in office for onboarding
- **Performance Issues**: May require increased office presence per manager discretion

## Meeting Norms

### Timezone Accommodation
- **Core Collaboration Hours**: 11 AM - 1 PM EST (8 AM - 10 AM PST, 4 PM - 6 PM CET)
- **All-Hands Meetings**: Fixed at 12 PM EST monthly (predictable compromise time)
- **EU Accommodation**: Maximum 1 early/late meeting per person per week

### Meeting Structure
- **Default Mode**: Hybrid-optimized (assume some participants are remote)
- **Technology Standard**: All meetings start with video/audio check
- **Documentation**: Meeting notes required within 48 hours
- **Recording Policy**: Available upon request for strategic meetings

### Critical In-Person Meetings
1. Quarterly business planning and reviews
2. Performance reviews and career development discussions
3. Client presentations and deal closings (Sales)
4. New employee onboarding sessions
5. Cross-functional project kickoffs

## Equipment and Technology Policy

### Company-Provided Equipment (All Employees)
- Laptop with docking station
- External monitor (24" minimum)
- Noise-canceling headphones
- Ergonomic keyboard and mouse

### Home Office Support
- **One-time Allowance**: $1,000 for desk and chair setup
- **Internet Stipend**: $50/month for upgraded internet
- **Equipment Ownership**: Company retains ownership; return required upon departure

### Office Resources
- **Desk Assignment**: Sales team has assigned desks; others use available workspace
- **Booking System**: Conference rooms bookable 1 week in advance
- **Priority System**: Sales team gets Tuesday-Thursday priority; others get Monday/Friday priority

### IT Support
- **Remote Support**: Available 8 AM - 6 PM EST weekdays
- **Equipment Replacement**: 48-hour turnaround for critical failures
- **VPN and Security**: Mandatory for all remote connections

## Performance Evaluation Approach

### Core Evaluation Principles
- **Output-Based Metrics**: Focus on deliverables and results, not hours or location
- **Regular Check-ins**: Monthly 1:1s regardless of work location
- **Consistent Standards**: Same expectations regardless of work mode

### Department-Specific Focus

#### Engineering
- Code quality and sprint commitment achievement
- Technical documentation and knowledge sharing
- Cross-team collaboration effectiveness

#### Sales
- Revenue targets and pipeline management
- Client relationship development
- Team collaboration and mentorship

#### All Functions
- Goal achievement and deadline adherence
- Communication effectiveness across work modes
- Professional development progress

### Performance Review Process
- **Frequency**: Quarterly check-ins, annual formal review
- **Format**: Video-first for consistency across locations
- **Documentation**: Structured feedback with specific examples

## Timezone Management

### Communication Expectations
- **Response Times**: 
  - Urgent: 4 hours during business hours
  - Standard: 24 hours
  - Non-urgent: 48 hours
- **After-Hours Policy**: No expectation of immediate response outside local business hours

### EU Team Integration
- **Meeting Participation**: Virtual attendance at all company meetings
- **Local Support**: EU team works with local vendors for equipment/IT support
- **Connection**: Monthly EU-focused session during EU-friendly hours
- **Travel**: Optional annual US office visit (company-sponsored but not required)

## Implementation and Compliance

### Enforcement Mechanism
- **Monitoring**: Monthly office utilization tracking
- **Non-Compliance**: Manager discussion after 30 days below requirements
- **Escalation**: HR involvement after repeated non-compliance
- **Flexibility**: Temporary adjustments for personal circumstances

### Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2): Policy Launch
- All-hands policy presentation
- Department-specific Q&A sessions
- Equipment ordering and setup

### Phase 2 (Weeks 3-6): Pilot Period
- Flexible enforcement with feedback collection
- Technology troubleshooting and optimization

### Phase 3 (Week 7+): Full Implementation
- Policy compliance monitoring begins
- Monthly feedback collection and refinement

## Budget Considerations

### Estimated Annual Costs
- Home office allowances: $50,000 one-time
- Internet stipends: $30,000 annually
- Office space: Current lease maintained
- EU support: $15,000 annually for local services

## Success Metrics

### Primary Measures
- Employee satisfaction with work arrangement (quarterly survey, target >4.0/5.0)
- Achievement of department-specific goals
- Office space utilization rates
- Employee retention rates

### Review Process
- **Monthly**: Utilization metrics and satisfaction data review
- **Quarterly**: Policy effectiveness assessment with department heads
- **Annually**: Comprehensive policy review with stakeholder input

## Policy Governance

### Amendment Process
- **Minor Changes**: Department head approval with HR consultation
- **Major Changes**: Leadership team approval with 30-day advance notice
- **Emergency Adjustments**: CEO approval with team notification
