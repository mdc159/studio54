## What SecureCode AI Should NEVER Claim

### ❌ **Prohibited Claims (Legal & Credibility Risks)**

1. **"100% security guarantee" or "Unhackable"**
   - *Risk:* No security solution can guarantee perfect protection
   - *Alternative:* "Eliminates the primary attack vector of cloud-based AI tools"

2. **"Replaces human security experts"**
   - *Risk:* Alienates key decision-makers and creates unrealistic expectations
   - *Alternative:* "Augments human expertise with AI-powered insights"

3. **"Faster than cloud solutions" (performance)**
   - *Risk:* Cloud solutions may have latency advantages
   - *Alternative:* "Optimal performance within your security requirements"

4. **"Works for any organization"**
   - *Risk:* Dilutes our focused positioning
   - *Alternative:* "Purpose-built for security-conscious enterprises"

5. **"No maintenance required"**
   - *Risk:* On-premise solutions inherently require maintenance
   - *Alternative:* "Comprehensive support and maintenance services included"

6. **"AI that thinks like humans"**
   - *Risk:* Overpromising on AI capabilities
   - *Alternative:* "AI trained to recognize patterns human reviewers look for"

7. **"Compliance certification guarantee"**
   - *Risk:* Compliance depends on implementation and customer environment
   - *Alternative:* "Designed to support your compliance requirements"

### ✅ **Safe Harbor Language Framework**

**Security Claims:**
