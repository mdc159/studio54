# HYBRID WORK POLICY
## Balancing Flexibility with Collaboration

### EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This policy establishes a flexible hybrid framework that respects Engineering's remote-first preference while addressing Sales' collaboration needs. The approach centers on outcomes over presence, with structured collaboration windows and role-specific flexibility.

---

## 1. REQUIRED IN-OFFICE PRESENCE

### Core Framework: "2+2+1" Model

**Engineering Team (20 people) - Remote-First Track**
- **Minimum requirement:** 2 days per month in office
- **Recommended:** 1 day per week when possible
- **Mandatory presence:** Quarterly planning sessions (2 days) and monthly all-hands
- **Flexibility:** Can bank monthly days (e.g., 4 days one month, 0 the next)

**Sales Team (15 people) - Collaboration Track**  
- **Minimum requirement:** 3 days per week in office (Tuesday-Thursday core)
- **Flexible days:** Monday and Friday can be remote
- **Mandatory presence:** Weekly sales meetings (Wednesdays), monthly pipeline reviews

**Leadership & Other Functions (15 people) - Hybrid Track**
- **Minimum requirement:** 2 days per week in office
- **Recommended:** Tuesday-Thursday for maximum overlap with both teams
- **Mandatory presence:** Monthly leadership meetings, quarterly reviews

### Office Hours & Coordination
- **Core office days:** Tuesday-Thursday (maximum overlap)
- **Quiet focus days:** Monday-Friday (reduced interruptions for deep work)
- **Team social events:** Last Thursday of each month (encouraged attendance)

---

## 2. MEETING NORMS

### Meeting Equity Standards

**All meetings must be "remote-first" regardless of attendee location:**
- Every meeting has a video link, even if some attendees are in-office
- In-office attendees must join from individual devices (no conference room huddling)
- All materials shared digitally in real-time
- Recordings mandatory for meetings with EU participants

### Meeting Types & Requirements

**Synchronous Meetings**
- **Engineering standups:** Async-first via Slack, optional 30-min sync twice weekly
- **Sales huddles:** Daily 15-min at 9 AM PT/12 PM ET (EU joins async)
- **Cross-team collaboration:** Scheduled within core overlap hours
- **One-on-ones:** Manager's discretion, monthly minimum requirement

**Asynchronous Communication**
- **Decision documentation:** All decisions recorded in Notion within 24 hours
- **Status updates:** Weekly written updates required from all team leads
- **Project updates:** Bi-weekly async updates for cross-team projects

### Core Collaboration Hours
- **US overlap window:** 12 PM - 3 PM ET (9 AM - 12 PM PT)
- **EU inclusion window:** 9 AM - 11 AM ET (3 PM - 5 PM CET) - twice weekly
- **No-meeting blocks:** Before 9 AM PT and after 6 PM ET

## 3. EQUIPMENT & TECHNOLOGY POLICY

### Standard Equipment Package (All Employees)

**Computing Equipment**
- MacBook Pro 14" or equivalent Windows laptop
- External monitor (27" minimum) for home office
- Ergonomic keyboard and mouse
- Laptop stand or docking station

**Communication & Collaboration**
- Noise-canceling headphones with microphone
- 1080p webcam (if laptop camera insufficient)
- Desk lamp with adjustable lighting for video calls

**Home Office Stipend**
- $1,200 annual allowance for desk, chair, and office setup
- $100/month internet reimbursement for remote-first employees
- $50/month phone stipend for all employees

### Office Equipment Standards
- Hot-desking stations with monitors and peripherals
- Dedicated desks for employees in office 3+ days/week
- Phone booths and focus rooms bookable via app
- High-quality conference room AV equipment

### Security Requirements
- VPN mandatory for all remote work
- Two-factor authentication on all company accounts
- Company-issued security keys for engineering team
- Monthly security training (15-minute async modules)

## 4. PERFORMANCE EVALUATION APPROACH

### Outcome-Based Performance Framework

**Core Principle:** Performance measured by results and impact, not location or hours worked.

### Evaluation Criteria (Weighted)

**Individual Contribution (60%)**
- Goal achievement and OKR completion
- Quality of work output and deliverables
- Technical/functional skill demonstration
- Innovation and problem-solving impact

**Collaboration & Communication (25%)**
- Cross-team project participation
- Knowledge sharing and documentation
- Responsiveness during core collaboration hours
- Mentoring and team support activities

**Cultural Alignment (15%)**
- Participation in team building (virtual and in-person)
- Adherence to hybrid work policy
- Proactive communication and transparency
- Support for company values and mission

### Review Process

**Quarterly Check-ins**
- 360-degree feedback including remote/in-office perspectives
- Self-assessment of hybrid work effectiveness
- Goal adjustment based on location and role needs

**Annual Reviews**
- Performance data aggregated across all quarters
- Location preference assessment and adjustment
- Career development planning with hybrid considerations
- Compensation review independent of work location

### Performance Support
- **Struggling remote workers:** Additional check-ins, co-working days, mentorship
- **Struggling office workers:** Flexible arrangements, focus time, productivity coaching
- **High performers:** Location flexibility rewards, conference attendance, equipment upgrades

## 5. TIMEZONE COORDINATION STRATEGY

### EU Team Integration (3 people)

**Communication Windows**
- **Morning sync:** 8:30 AM - 9:30 AM ET (2:30 PM - 3:30 PM CET)
- **Afternoon handoff:** 11 AM - 12 PM ET (5 PM - 6 PM CET)
- **Weekly EU focus time:** Tuesdays 4 AM - 8 AM PT (EU afternoon)

**EU-Specific Accommodations**
- Rotating meeting times monthly (early US / late EU alternating)
- Async decision-making processes with 24-hour response windows
- EU team lead designated for local coordination
- Quarterly in-person EU meetup (company-funded)

### Cross-Coast US Coordination

**Shared Core Hours:** 12 PM - 3 PM ET / 9 AM - 12 PM PT
- All critical meetings within this window
- Engineering code reviews and pair programming
- Sales pipeline reviews and client calls
- Cross-functional project coordination

**Coast-Specific Flexibility**
- East Coast: Early meetings acceptable (8 AM ET start)
- West Coast: Late meetings acceptable (6 PM PT end)
- Documentation handoffs for asynchronous work
- Follow-the-sun development practices for engineering

### Timezone Equity Measures

**Meeting Rotation Schedule**
- Monthly rotation of recurring meeting times
- EU-friendly slots: 1st week of each month
- West Coast friendly: 3rd week of each month
- Standard time: 2nd and 4th weeks

**Communication Standards**
- Response time expectations: 24 hours for non-urgent, 4 hours during local business hours
- Urgent escalation process via Slack/phone for immediate needs
- Weekly async updates to bridge timezone gaps
- Shared calendar with all timezone displays

## IMPLEMENTATION TIMELINE

### Phase 1 (Months 1-2): Foundation
- Equipment ordering and home office setup
- Policy communication and training sessions
- Office space reconfiguration for hot-desking
- Technology stack implementation (booking systems, collaboration tools)

### Phase 2 (Months 3-4): Pilot Program
- Trial run with voluntary participation
- Weekly feedback sessions and policy adjustments
- Performance evaluation baseline establishment
- Timezone coordination process refinement

### Phase 3 (Months 5-6): Full Deployment
- Mandatory policy implementation
- First quarterly performance reviews under new system
- Policy effectiveness assessment
- Long-term optimization planning

### Quarterly Assessment Criteria
- Employee satisfaction scores by team and location preference
- Productivity metrics (engineering velocity, sales pipeline conversion)
- Collaboration effectiveness (cross-team project success, meeting engagement)
- Retention rates and recruitment success
- Cost analysis (office space, equipment, travel)

- Monthly pulse surveys (5 questions, anonymous)
- Quarterly policy adjustment sessions
- Bi-annual comprehensive policy review
