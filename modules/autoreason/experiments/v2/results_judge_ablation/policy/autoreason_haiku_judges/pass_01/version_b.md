MEMORANDUM

SUBJECT: Artificial Intelligence Tool Usage Policy

1. SCOPE
   1.1 This policy applies to all employees of [Company Name]
   1.2 Covers AI tools used for work-related tasks, specifically:
      a) Code generation and assistance
      b) Text generation and editing
      c) Analytical and productivity tools
   1.3 Effective immediately upon distribution
   1.4 Supersedes all previous informal practices regarding AI tool usage

2. PERMITTED USES
   2.1 Authorized AI Tools:
      a) GitHub Copilot Business (code generation)
      b) Approved list maintained by IT and Legal departments
   2.2 Permitted AI tool usage requires:
      a) Explicit tool-specific training
      b) Documented review process for generated content
      c) Verification of copyright and ownership status
   2.3 Employees must:
      a) Modify AI-generated content substantively
      b) Ensure generated content meets professional standards
      c) Document AI tool usage and review process

3. PROHIBITED USES
   3.1 Prohibited Actions:
      a) Inputting customer PII into external AI services (Ref: Base Fact #2, Incident #1)
      b) Generating complete work products without manual review
      c) Using unapproved AI tools for work tasks
      d) Inputting proprietary company intellectual property into external services
      e) Accepting AI-generated content with unverified licensing
   3.2 Compliance Violations:
      a) Actions compromising SOC2, GDPR, or FedRAMP compliance
      b) Bypassing established review and approval processes

4. ENFORCEMENT
   4.1 Violation Response Framework:
      a) First Violation: Written warning, mandatory retraining
      b) Second Violation: Performance improvement plan
      c) Third Violation: Potential termination
   4.2 Documentation Requirements:
      a) Managers must document and report potential violations
      b) IT will maintain quarterly compliance audit logs
      c) Violations tracked in employee personnel records
   4.3 Compliance Oversight:
      a) Quarterly review of approved AI tools by IT and Legal
      b) Annual policy and tool usage assessment
      c) Immediate review triggered by:
         - Significant tool functionality changes
         - Emerging legal or security concerns
   4.4 Reporting Mechanism:
      a) Employees must report potential violations to manager or HR
      b) Confidential reporting channel established
   4.5 Compliance Responsibilities:
      a) Departmental AI Compliance Liaisons appointed
      b) Annual mandatory AI tool usage training
