## WHAT SECURECOCE AI SHOULD NEVER CLAIM TO BE

### ❌ Avoid These Positioning Statements:

1. **"A replacement for human code reviewers"**
   - *Why*: Creates fear and resistance among engineering teams
   - *Instead*: "Augments and accelerates human expertise"

2. **"100% accurate vulnerability detection"**
   - *Why*: Sets unrealistic expectations and creates liability
   - *Instead*: "Significantly improves detection rates with human oversight"

3. **"Easy plug-and-play installation"**
   - *Why*: Understates enterprise deployment complexity
   - *Instead*: "Enterprise-grade deployment with comprehensive support"

4. **"Cheaper than cloud alternatives"**
   - *Why*: Commoditizes the solution and ignores value proposition
   - *Instead*: "Better TCO when security and compliance costs are included"

5. **"AI that learns automatically from your code"**
   - *Why*: Raises privacy concerns even in on-premise scenarios
   - *Instead*: "Configurable learning with full transparency and control"

6. **"Works with any programming language"**
   - *Why*: Implies lack of specialization
   - *Instead*: "Optimized for enterprise languages with expanding coverage"
