**Response Framework:**
- **Acknowledge:** "You're right that on-premise solutions typically require more infrastructure investment upfront."
- **Reframe:** "However, consider the total cost of a security breach or compliance violation. Our customers typically see ROI within 12-18 months through reduced security incidents and faster compliance audits."
- **Simplify:** "Our containerized deployment reduces operational overhead by 70% compared to traditional on-premise solutions, and we provide full deployment support."

### Objection: "How do we know the AI quality matches cloud solutions?"

- **Realistic Expectations:** "AI code review isn't about replacing human judgment - it's about catching common security issues that humans miss under time pressure."
- **Proof Approach:** "We offer a 90-day proof-of-concept on your actual codebase so you can see the results firsthand."
