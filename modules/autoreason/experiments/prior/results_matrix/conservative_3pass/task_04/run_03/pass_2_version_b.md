## What SecureCode AI Should NEVER Claim

### Avoid These Claims:

1. **"Dedicated infrastructure for each customer"**
   - Risk: Economically unviable and operationally impossible
   - Instead: "Enhanced security controls within enterprise-grade infrastructure"

2. **"Runs on your infrastructure"**
   - Risk: Understates complexity and creates impossible expectations
   - Instead: "Secure cloud deployment with enhanced enterprise controls"

3. **"Same cost as basic solutions"**
   - Risk: Sets unrealistic pricing expectations that break unit economics
   - Instead: "Premium pricing for enhanced security features and enterprise support"

4. **"Superior AI to market leaders"**
   - Risk: Easily disprovable and damages credibility
   - Instead: "Comparable AI capabilities with enhanced security features"

5. **"Compliance requirements mandate our solution"**
   - Risk: Creates fictional regulatory requirements
   - Instead: "Enhanced security features for security-conscious organizations"

6. **"18-36 month sales cycles are normal"**
   - Risk: Makes business model economically impossible
   - Instead: "6-12 month evaluation process with security validation"
