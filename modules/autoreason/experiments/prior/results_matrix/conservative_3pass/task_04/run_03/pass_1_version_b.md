## What SecureCode AI Should NEVER Claim

### Avoid These Claims:

1. **"Air-gapped deployment" or "never touches the internet"**
   - Risk: Technically impossible for modern AI systems requiring updates
   - Instead: "Dedicated cloud infrastructure with comprehensive security controls"

2. **"Runs on your existing infrastructure"**
   - Risk: Understates massive infrastructure requirements for enterprise AI
   - Instead: "Managed service with dedicated infrastructure"

3. **"Same cost as cloud solutions"**
   - Risk: Sets unrealistic pricing expectations
   - Instead: "Premium pricing justified by enterprise security controls and compliance benefits"

4. **"Better AI than OpenAI/GitHub"**
   - Risk: Easily disprovable and damages credibility
   - Instead: "Comparable AI capabilities with enterprise security controls"

5. **"Instant deployment"**
   - Risk: Ignores reality of enterprise security assessments
   - Instead: "Comprehensive deployment process including security validation"

6. **"No internet connectivity required"**
   - Risk: Technically impossible for model updates and management
   - Instead: "Secure, controlled connectivity with comprehensive audit trails"
