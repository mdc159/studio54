## What SecureCode AI Should NEVER Claim

### Avoid These Claims:

1. **"Pre-built compliance documentation" or "guaranteed regulatory approval"**
   - Risk: Compliance is organization-specific and regulators make their own decisions
   - Instead: "Custom compliance consulting and documentation support"

2. **"Same performance as cloud AI solutions"**
   - Risk: Dedicated infrastructure may have performance trade-offs
   - Instead: "Enterprise-grade AI performance with regulatory compliance"

3. **"Multi-tenant enhanced security" or "shared infrastructure with isolation"**
   - Risk: Contradicts the fundamental value proposition of dedicated infrastructure
   - Instead: "Single-tenant dedicated infrastructure with contractual guarantees"

4. **"Suitable for all enterprises" or "flexible deployment options"**
   - Risk: Dilutes positioning and creates unrealistic expectations
   - Instead: "Purpose-built for regulated enterprises requiring dedicated infrastructure"

*[Fixes Problem 1 & 2: Eliminates contradictory technical claims and market positioning]*
