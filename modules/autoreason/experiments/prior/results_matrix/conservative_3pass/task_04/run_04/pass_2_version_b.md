## Executive Summary

SecureCode AI provides **AI-powered code review for air-gapped and classified environments** where cloud connectivity is architecturally impossible. We serve a small but critical market: organizations operating in classified, SCIF, or completely isolated network environments where no external connectivity is permitted.

Our core value proposition: **"AI code review for environments that cannot connect to the internet—purpose-built for air-gapped operations."**

**Market Reality:** This is a niche market of approximately 80-120 organizations globally, primarily in classified government work, defense contractors with SCIF environments, and critical infrastructure with mandatory air gaps. We serve customers who have no alternatives, not those who prefer on-premise solutions.

**FIXES:** Addresses market reality problems by dramatically reducing market size estimates and focusing on true air-gapped environments rather than general "on-premise preferences."
