## What SecureCodeAI Should NEVER Claim

### ❌ **Avoid These Claims:**

1. **"We're faster/better than GitHub Copilot"**
   - *Why:* Copilot has massive scale advantages and continuous training
   - *Instead:* Focus on security and customization benefits

2. **"Zero setup required"**
   - *Why:* On-premise inherently requires setup—don't minimize this reality
   - *Instead:* Emphasize comprehensive deployment support

3. **"We replace your security team"**
   - *Why:* Positions as replacement rather than enhancement
   - *Instead:* "Amplifies your security team's capabilities"

4. **"100% accurate code reviews"**
   - *Why:* No AI is perfect—sets unrealistic expectations
   - *Instead:* "Significantly improves review consistency and coverage"

5. **"Works with any codebase immediately"**
   - *Why:* Custom training and configuration are part of our value
   - *Instead:* "Optimizes over time for your specific development patterns"

6. **"Cheaper than cloud alternatives"**
   - *Why:* Upfront costs are typically higher
   - *Instead:* Focus on total cost of ownership including risk mitigation

### ❌ **Avoid These Positioning Mistakes:**

- **Don't lead with features:** Start with business outcomes (security, compliance)
- **Don't apologize for being on-premise:** Frame it as a competitive advantage
- **Don't compare on generic capabilities:** Focus on security-specific differentiators
- **Don't promise cloud-like convenience:** Emphasize enterprise-grade reliability instead
