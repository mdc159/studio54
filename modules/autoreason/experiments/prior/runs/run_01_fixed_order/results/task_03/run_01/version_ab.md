# HYBRID WORK POLICY (SYNTHESIZED)
## Effective Date: [Insert Date]

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This policy establishes a flexible hybrid framework with department-specific requirements and unified core collaboration days. Engineering maintains remote flexibility with strategic touchpoints, Sales operates with stronger office presence, and all departments align on Wednesdays for cross-functional collaboration. The policy prioritizes business outcomes while ensuring operational simplicity and equitable treatment.

---

## 1. REQUIRED IN-OFFICE PRESENCE

### 1.1 Department-Specific Requirements with Universal Core Day

**Engineering Team (20 people)**
- **Minimum requirement**: 1 day per month in-office
- **Universal core day**: Wednesday (cross-functional collaboration)
- **Department core day**: Second Tuesday of each month (all-hands engineering)
- **Sprint planning**: In-person preferred for major releases (quarterly)

**Sales Team (15 people)**
- **Minimum requirement**: 3 days per week in-office
- **Universal core day**: Wednesday (cross-functional collaboration)
- **Department core days**: Tuesday and Thursday
- **Client meetings**: In-person when client is local, otherwise team's discretion

**Leadership/Other Functions (15 people)**
- **Minimum requirement**: 2 days per week in-office
- **Universal core day**: Wednesday (leadership and cross-functional alignment)
- **Flexible day**: One additional day based on team needs

*Rationale for departure from Version A: Added universal Wednesday core day from Version B to fix fragmented office population while preserving department-specific optimization from Version A. This ensures cross-functional collaboration without destroying Engineering's remote-first effectiveness.*

### 1.2 Exceptions and Accommodations
- **New hires**: First 30 days require 50% in-office presence regardless of department
- **Performance improvement plans**: May require increased office presence
- **Medical/family accommodations**: Case-by-case review with HR
- **EU employees**: Follow same department requirements using designated coworking spaces

## 2. OFFICE LOGISTICS AND COLLABORATION

### 2.1 Workspace Allocation

**Hybrid Desk System**
- **Engineering**: Hot-desking with 24-hour advance booking (low utilization)
- **Sales/Leadership**: Assigned desks (high utilization)
- **Shared spaces**: Team collaboration areas available on Wednesday core days

*Rationale for departure from Version A: Adopted Version B's insight about hot-desking limitations but applied it selectively. Sales teams need assigned desks for consistent presence, while Engineering's low utilization makes hot-desking workable.*

### 2.2 Meeting Standards

**Meeting Technology and Guidelines**
- **Primary hybrid rooms**: 2 rooms per floor with quality audio/video for cross-functional meetings
- **Standard rooms**: Basic video conferencing for department meetings
- **Wednesday meetings**: In-person preferred for cross-functional collaboration
- **Department meetings**: Follow department-specific norms (Engineering virtual-first, Sales in-person)

*Rationale for departure from Version A: Adopted Version B's realistic technology approach but maintained Version A's department-specific meeting norms, focusing investment on cross-functional spaces.*

### 2.3 Communication Protocols
- **Synchronous communication**: Slack for urgent matters (4-hour response expectation)
- **Asynchronous communication**: Email for non-urgent items (24-hour response)
- **Documentation**: All decisions documented in shared systems
- **Cross-timezone meetings**: Scheduled during 9 AM - 12 PM PT overlap window
