# REVISED HYBRID WORK POLICY
**Effective Date: [Date]**  
**Version 2.0**

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This policy establishes a simplified hybrid framework with two primary work modes: **Office-Required** roles and **Location-Flexible** roles. Clear guidelines ensure operational efficiency while maintaining cost control and legal compliance.

---

## 1. WORK LOCATION REQUIREMENTS

### 1.1 Role-Based Classification

**Office-Required Roles** (Sales, Customer Success, Facilities)
- **Minimum office days:** 3 days per week (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday)
- **Core hours:** 9 AM - 5 PM local time
- **Remote work:** Permitted for client travel and approved business needs

**Location-Flexible Roles** (Engineering, Marketing, Finance, HR)
- **Minimum office days:** 1 day per week (choose from Tuesday-Thursday)
- **Core collaboration hours:** 10 AM - 3 PM PT for all US employees
- **Geographic restriction:** Must work from approved US states only

### 1.2 Company Collaboration Days
- **Core Collaboration Day:** Wednesday - all local employees in office
- **Quarterly All-Hands:** In-person attendance required for all local employees

*FIXES: Eliminates complex department-specific rules and rotating schedules that created operational chaos. Removes EU accommodation that was disproportionate for 3 employees.*

---

## 2. MEETING AND COMMUNICATION STANDARDS

### 2.1 Meeting Guidelines
- **Default format:** Hybrid-capable (quality A/V in all conference rooms)
- **Video requirement:** Cameras on for all participants (remote and in-office)
- **Documentation:** Meeting notes and decisions recorded in shared workspace
- **Scheduling:** All meetings during 10 AM - 3 PM PT core hours

### 2.2 Communication Response Standards
- **Urgent:** Slack with direct message, 2-hour response expected
- **Standard:** Email or Slack, 24-hour response expected (business days only)
- **Cross-team coordination:** Weekly async updates via shared dashboards

*FIXES: Eliminates complex recording requirements and storage costs. Simplifies timezone coordination by focusing on US-only operations.*

---

## 3. EQUIPMENT AND WORKSPACE POLICY

### 3.1 Equipment Allocation

**All Employees Receive:**
- Company laptop and basic accessories
- Home monitor (single 24" display)
- $300 annual home office stipend for desk setup/internet

**Office-Required Roles Additionally Receive:**
- Assigned desk with dual monitors
- Office phone and headset

**Location-Flexible Roles Use:**
- Hot-desking stations when in office
- Shared conference room booking system

### 3.2 Equipment Management
- Equipment ships to primary work address only
- IT support via ticket system with 24-hour response SLA
- Annual equipment refresh based on depreciation schedule

*FIXES: Reduces equipment costs from $2,700 to $300 annually per remote employee. Eliminates unworkable 4-hour remote SLA. Creates realistic hot-desking model.*

---

## 4. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT

### 4.1 Evaluation Principles
- **Results-based assessment:** Focus on deliverables and goal achievement
- **Standardized metrics:** Department-specific KPIs applied equally regardless of location
- **Regular check-ins:** Monthly 1:1s with manager (virtual or in-person)

### 4.2 Performance Tracking
- **Quarterly reviews:** Goal progress and peer feedback
- **Annual assessment:** Comprehensive review with promotion/compensation decisions
- **Success metrics:** Individual OKRs and team contribution measures

*FIXES: Removes contradictory output vs. location tracking. Simplifies evaluation process while maintaining fairness.*

---

## 5. OPERATIONAL COMPLIANCE

### 5.1 Attendance Tracking
- **Office days:** Badge entry tracked for compliance reporting only
- **Core hours:** Calendar availability during required collaboration time
- **Exception process:** Manager approval for schedule deviations, documented monthly

### 5.2 Geographic and Legal Requirements
- **Approved locations:** US states where company maintains employment compliance
- **International work:** Not permitted under this policy
- **Home office requirements:** Employees certify safe workspace setup annually

*FIXES: Eliminates complex international compliance issues. Creates simple but enforceable tracking system.*

---

## 6. COST MANAGEMENT AND BUDGETING

### 6.1 Office Space Planning
- **Desk ratio:** 1.2 desks per office-required employee + 0.3 per location-flexible employee
- **Space optimization:** Hot-desking areas sized for actual usage patterns
- **Capacity management:** Desk booking system with 48-hour advance notice

### 6.2 Remote Work Cost Analysis
- **Annual cost per remote employee:** $300 stipend + equipment depreciation
- **Office cost savings:** Reduced square footage requirements based on actual occupancy
- **Budget review:** Quarterly assessment of space needs vs. remote work costs

*FIXES: Provides realistic cost model and space planning. Eliminates excessive remote work stipends.*

---

## 7. IMPLEMENTATION PLAN

### 7.1 30-Day Transition
- **Week 1:** Role classification and employee notification
- **Week 2:** Equipment distribution and office desk assignments
- **Week 3:** Manager training on hybrid team management
- **Week 4:** Full policy implementation with feedback collection

### 7.2 Success Measurement
- **Operational metrics:** Meeting attendance, project completion rates, response times
- **Employee feedback:** Quarterly survey on work effectiveness and satisfaction
- **Business impact:** Customer satisfaction scores, revenue per employee, retention rates

### 7.3 Policy Governance
- **Monthly review:** Department head feedback on operational issues
- **Quarterly adjustment:** Policy modifications based on success metrics
- **Annual assessment:** Comprehensive cost-benefit analysis and policy update

*FIXES: Provides realistic implementation timeline. Includes manager training that was missing. Creates measurable success criteria.*

---

## 8. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Can I work from another state while traveling?**
A: Occasional travel (under 30 days annually) is permitted. Extended work from unapproved states requires legal review.

**Q: What if I'm assigned to Office-Required but want Location-Flexible?**
A: Role classification is based on business needs. Discuss with manager during annual review for potential role changes.

**Q: How do I request a desk for hot-desking?**
A: Use the shared booking calendar. Desks are available first-come, first-served within 48 hours of booking.

**Q: What happens if someone consistently misses core collaboration hours?**
A: Addressed through standard performance management process with documented expectations.
