# HYBRID WORK POLICY - REVISED
## Effective Date: [Date] | Version 2.0

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This policy establishes clear work location requirements based on actual business needs while maintaining flexibility where it doesn't impact collaboration. Rather than creating complex frameworks, we focus on three simple principles: client-facing work happens where clients need it, collaborative work happens when teams can effectively work together, and individual work can happen anywhere.

---

## 1. WORK LOCATION REQUIREMENTS

### 1.1 Department-Specific Requirements

**Engineering Team (20 people)**
- **No minimum office requirement** - work location based on project needs
- **Required office presence:** Only when attending scheduled collaborative sessions (sprint planning, architecture reviews, all-hands)
- **Manager discretion:** Team leads can request specific office days for project kickoffs or problem-solving sessions

*Fixes: Eliminates performative "1 day per month" requirement that provides no business value*

**Sales Team (15 people)**
- **Minimum requirement:** In office when scheduled client meetings require in-person presence
- **Team coordination:** Monday team meetings in office (9 AM EST), other days flexible based on client needs
- **Client-first principle:** Work location determined by client meeting schedules, not arbitrary day patterns

*Fixes: Replaces rigid Tue/Wed/Thu pattern with client-driven scheduling that matches actual work needs*

**Leadership/Other Functions (15 people)**
- **Required office presence:** Monthly all-hands meetings and during their direct reports' required office days
- **Flexible scheduling:** Other office days based on meeting schedules and team needs

*Fixes: Removes arbitrary "2 days per week" requirement, focuses on actual leadership presence needs*

### 1.2 Collaboration Requirements

**Scheduled Collaborative Sessions**
- **All-hands meetings:** Monthly, first Wednesday, 11 AM EST (hybrid format)
- **Sprint planning:** Engineering team choice of format, scheduled 2 weeks in advance
- **Client handoffs:** Format determined by client requirements and urgency

**Daily Coordination**
- **No required overlap hours** - teams coordinate based on project deadlines
- **Response time expectations:** 4 hours for urgent requests (defined as impacting client deliverables or blocking other team members), 24 hours for standard requests

*Fixes: Eliminates unworkable "core hours" that created scheduling conflicts across timezones*

---

## 2. COMMUNICATION AND MEETING STANDARDS

### 2.1 Meeting Format Guidelines

**Default Format Decision Tree:**
- **Client meetings:** Format determined by client preference and relationship stage
- **Internal meetings:** Default to most convenient format for majority of required participants
- **Urgent problem-solving:** Phone calls preferred for speed, documentation required afterward

**Technology Standards:**
- **Conference rooms:** Standard video conferencing setup (existing equipment only)
- **Individual responsibility:** Each employee ensures they have adequate home setup for video calls
- **Recording:** Meetings recorded only when specifically requested and when all participants consent

*Fixes: Eliminates expensive 360-degree camera requirements and unenforceable "no sidebar" rules*

### 2.2 Documentation Requirements

**Decision Documentation:**
- **Client decisions:** Sales team documents in CRM within same business day
- **Engineering decisions:** Technical decisions documented in existing project management tools
- **Policy/process changes:** HR documents and communicates within 48 hours

*Fixes: Removes arbitrary 24-hour rule, focuses on business-critical documentation*

---

## 3. EQUIPMENT AND SUPPORT

### 3.1 Standard Equipment

**All Employees:**
- Laptop and basic peripherals (monitor, keyboard, mouse, headset)
- **Home office stipend:** $300 one-time allowance with receipt submission, no pre-approval required
- **Internet reimbursement:** $50/month for employees working from home >50% of time

*Fixes: Reduces administrative overhead while providing necessary support; eliminates unauditable $500 allowances*

### 3.2 Office Resources

**Flexible Office Space:**
- **Desk reservation:** Simple first-come, first-served for available desks
- **Conference rooms:** Bookable through existing calendar system
- **Dedicated spaces:** Sales team members who need daily office presence can request assigned desks

*Fixes: Eliminates need for dedicated office management platform and staff*

---

## 4. PERFORMANCE AND CAREER DEVELOPMENT

### 4.1 Evaluation Approach

**Performance Standards:**
- **Individual contributors:** Measured on delivery of assigned work and responsiveness to team coordination needs
- **Client-facing roles:** Measured on client satisfaction and revenue/project outcomes
- **Managers:** Include "remote team effectiveness" in leadership evaluation based on team delivery and retention

**Location Neutrality:**
- **Promotion eligibility:** Based on role requirements and performance, not office presence
- **No location tracking:** Managers focus on work output and team collaboration, not attendance monitoring

*Fixes: Eliminates complex calibration sessions and unworkable 360-degree feedback systems*

---

## 5. TIMEZONE COORDINATION

### 5.1 EU Team Integration (3 people)

**Meeting Scheduling:**
- **EU participation:** EU team attends meetings during their 9 AM - 12 PM local time (US morning)
- **Async updates:** EU team provides written updates for meetings outside their hours
- **Direct communication:** EU team has direct access to US team members via Slack/email with 24-hour response expectation

**Quarterly Connection:**
- **Video team building:** One quarterly video call scheduled for EU-friendly hours
- **No required travel:** US office visits optional and individually requested

*Fixes: Eliminates expensive quarterly travel requirement and complex meeting rotation schedules*

---

## 6. IMPLEMENTATION

### 6.1 30-Day Implementation
- **Week 1:** Equipment orders and office space assignments
- **Week 2-3:** Team meetings to establish specific collaboration schedules
- **Week 4:** Full policy in effect

### 6.2 Success Measurement
- **Client satisfaction scores** (for sales team changes)
- **Project delivery timelines** (for engineering flexibility)
- **Employee retention rates** by department
- **Office space utilization** for cost optimization

*Fixes: Focuses on business outcomes rather than feel-good surveys*

### 6.3 Policy Adjustments
- **Quarterly review:** Department heads report what's working/not working
- **Changes:** Simple majority vote by leadership team, implemented within 2 weeks
- **Conflicts:** Department heads work directly with affected employees, escalate to CEO only if no resolution within 1 week

*Fixes: Creates practical conflict resolution without complex escalation procedures*

---

## 7. MANAGER RESPONSIBILITIES

**Basic Requirements:**
- **Scheduling coordination:** Ensure team members know about required office days at least 1 week in advance
- **Performance focus:** Evaluate based on work output and team collaboration, not location
- **Communication:** Respond to team member questions about work location within 24 hours

**No Required Training:**
- Managers use existing skills and judgment
- HR available for consultation on specific situations

*Fixes: Eliminates need for expensive manager training program development*
