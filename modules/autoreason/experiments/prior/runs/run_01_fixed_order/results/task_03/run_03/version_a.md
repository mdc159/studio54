# HYBRID WORK POLICY
## Balancing Flexibility with Collaboration

**Effective Date:** [Implementation Date]  
**Policy Owner:** CEO & Head of People Operations  
**Review Cycle:** Quarterly for first year, then annually

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This policy establishes a flexible hybrid framework that respects engineering's need for deep work while enabling sales' collaboration requirements. The approach prioritizes outcomes over presence while maintaining team cohesion across three time zones.

---

## 1. OFFICE ATTENDANCE REQUIREMENTS

### Core Framework: "Anchor Days + Flex"

**Engineering Teams (20 people):**
- **Required:** 1 anchor day per week (Wednesdays)
- **Optional:** Up to 2 additional days based on project needs
- **Flexibility:** Can work fully remote during crunch periods with manager approval

**Sales Teams (15 people):**
- **Required:** 3 anchor days per week (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday)
- **Flexibility:** Monday/Friday remote unless client meetings require office presence
- **Exception:** Account managers may request modified schedules for client alignment

**All Other Roles (15 people):**
- **Required:** 2 anchor days per week (Wednesdays + one team-specific day)
- **Manager Discretion:** Additional days based on role requirements

### Special Circumstances
- **New Hires:** First 30 days require 4 days/week in office for onboarding
- **EU Team Members:** No office requirement; participate virtually in anchor day activities
- **Quarterly All-Hands:** Entire team required in office (EU team joins virtually)

---

## 2. MEETING NORMS & COLLABORATION STANDARDS

### Meeting Types & Formats

**Daily Standups:**
- Engineering: Async via Slack by 10 AM ET, video optional
- Sales: Live video at 9 AM ET/6 AM PT, mandatory attendance

**Weekly Team Meetings:**
- Must occur on designated anchor days when possible
- Hybrid format required (both in-person and remote participants)
- All meetings recorded for EU team review

**Cross-Functional Collaboration:**
- Engineering-Sales touchpoints: Bi-weekly, alternating time zones (9 AM ET one week, 12 PM ET next)
- Product reviews: Monthly, always on anchor days with full hybrid setup

### Meeting Technology Standards
- **Primary Platform:** Zoom with dedicated conference room setups
- **Documentation:** All decisions logged in Notion within 24 hours
- **EU Accommodation:** Critical meetings scheduled between 12-3 PM ET when possible

### Collaboration Windows
- **Core Hours:** 12 PM - 3 PM ET (overlap for all US time zones)
- **EU Sync Time:** 9 AM - 11 AM ET (3 PM - 5 PM CET)
- **Response Expectations:** 
  - Urgent: 2 hours during business hours
  - Standard: 24 hours
  - EU team: 48 hours for non-urgent items

## 3. EQUIPMENT & WORKSPACE POLICY

### Home Office Setup
**Standard Allocation (All Employees):**
- Laptop (company standard)
- External monitor (24" minimum)
- Ergonomic chair (up to $400)
- Desk setup allowance ($300)
- Noise-canceling headphones

**Engineering Additional Equipment:**
- Second monitor option
- Mechanical keyboard allowance ($200)
- Standing desk converter option

**Sales Additional Equipment:**
- Ring light for client video calls
- Professional backdrop
- Upgraded webcam

### Office Equipment
- **Hot-desking system** for non-anchor days
- **Assigned desks** available for employees requesting permanent office setup
- **Collaboration spaces** bookable via company system
- **Phone booths** for private calls

### Equipment Management
- **Shipping:** Company covers shipping for home office equipment
- **EU Team:** Local procurement with expense reimbursement (up to equivalent USD amounts)
- **Replacement:** 3-year refresh cycle, earlier if performance issues documented
- **Return Policy:** Equipment must be returned if employment ends or employee relocates beyond service area

## 4. PERFORMANCE EVALUATION APPROACH

### Evaluation Principles
1. **Results-focused:** Performance measured by outcomes, not hours or location
2. **Equity:** No preference given to office vs. remote work in evaluations
3. **Transparency:** Clear metrics established for all roles regardless of work location

### Performance Metrics Framework

**Engineering:**
- Code quality and review participation
- Sprint goal completion rates
- Technical documentation contributions
- Peer collaboration scores (via quarterly 360 reviews)

**Sales:**
- Revenue targets and pipeline metrics
- Client satisfaction scores
- Team collaboration effectiveness
- Knowledge sharing contributions

**Universal Metrics:**
- Goal achievement (OKRs)
- Communication effectiveness
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Professional development progress

### Evaluation Process
- **Quarterly Check-ins:** Focus on goal progress and support needs
- **Annual Reviews:** Comprehensive evaluation including 360 feedback
- **Location Bias Prevention:** Standardized rubrics with specific examples of success in both remote and office settings
- **EU Accommodation:** Performance conversations scheduled during EU-friendly hours when possible

### Manager Training Requirements
- Quarterly training on remote management best practices
- Bias awareness workshops
- Tools training for hybrid team management

---

## 5. TIMEZONE COORDINATION STRATEGY

### Communication Protocols

**Synchronous Communication:**
- **US Teams:** Core hours 12-3 PM ET for real-time collaboration
- **EU Integration:** Weekly EU-US sync meetings at 9 AM ET/3 PM CET
- **Urgent Escalation:** Clear handoff protocols for time-sensitive issues

**Asynchronous Communication:**
- **Documentation First:** All decisions and updates recorded in shared systems
- **Video Updates:** Weekly async video updates from each team for EU review
- **Slack Protocols:** Thread organization by timezone for easy catch-up

### Project Management Adaptations
- **Sprint Planning:** Includes EU input via async feedback before live sessions
- **Code Reviews:** 24-hour SLA to accommodate timezone differences
- **Client Handoffs:** Sales team maintains handoff documentation for EU support coverage

### Meeting Scheduling Guidelines
- **EU-Critical Meetings:** 8-11 AM ET window
- **US-Only Meetings:** 12-5 PM ET window
- **All-Hands:** Rotating times quarterly to share timezone burden
- **Recording Mandatory:** For any meeting where not all stakeholders can attend live

### EU Team Integration
- **Buddy System:** Each EU team member paired with US mentor for cultural/process support
- **Quarterly Visits:** Budget allocated for EU team members to visit US office
- **Local Networking:** Allowance for EU team to attend relevant local industry events
