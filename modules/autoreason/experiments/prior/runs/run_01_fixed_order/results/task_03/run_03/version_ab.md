# HYBRID WORK POLICY
## Balancing Flexibility with Collaboration

**Effective Date:** [Implementation Date]  
**Policy Owner:** CEO & Head of People Operations  
**Review Cycle:** 6-month pilot, then annually

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This policy establishes a flexible hybrid framework that respects engineering's need for deep work while enabling sales' collaboration requirements. The approach prioritizes outcomes over presence while maintaining team cohesion across three time zones, with disciplined cost controls and measurable success criteria.

---

## 1. OFFICE ATTENDANCE REQUIREMENTS

### Core Framework: "Anchor Days + Flex"

**Engineering Teams (20 people):**
- **Required:** 1 anchor day per week (Wednesdays)
- **Optional:** Up to 2 additional days based on project needs
- **Flexibility:** Can work fully remote during crunch periods with manager approval

**Sales Teams (15 people):**
- **Required:** 3 anchor days per week (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday)
- **Flexibility:** Monday/Friday remote unless client meetings require office presence
- **Exception:** Account managers may request modified schedules for client alignment

**All Other Roles (15 people):**
- **Required:** 2 anchor days per week (Wednesdays + one team-specific day)
- **Manager Discretion:** Additional days based on role requirements

### Special Circumstances
- **New Hires:** First 2 weeks in office for onboarding and system setup *(reduced from 30 days to control office crowding)*
- **EU Team Members:** No office requirement; participate virtually in anchor day activities
- **Quarterly All-Hands:** Entire team required in office (EU team joins virtually)

### Capacity Management
- **Office Capacity Limit:** 60% to ensure space availability and parking
- **Advance Booking Required:** 48-hour notice for office space beyond anchor days
- **Overflow Protocol:** Virtual participation when office capacity reached

---

## 2. MEETING NORMS & COLLABORATION STANDARDS

### Meeting Types & Formats

**Daily Standups:**
- Engineering: Async via Slack by 10 AM ET, video optional
- Sales: Live video at 9 AM ET/6 AM PT, mandatory attendance

**Weekly Team Meetings:**
- Must occur on designated anchor days when possible
- Hybrid format required (both in-person and remote participants)
- Recorded for EU team review *(limited to training sessions and all-hands to control storage costs)*

**Cross-Functional Collaboration:**
- Engineering-Sales touchpoints: Bi-weekly, alternating time zones (9 AM ET one week, 12 PM ET next)
- Product reviews: Monthly, always on anchor days with full hybrid setup

### Meeting Technology Standards
- **Primary Platform:** Zoom using existing conference room setup *(focused on main conference room to control infrastructure costs)*
- **Documentation:** All decisions logged in existing shared drive within 24 hours
- **EU Accommodation:** Critical meetings scheduled during 1-3 PM ET core overlap hours

### Collaboration Windows
- **Core Hours:** 1 PM - 3 PM ET (overlap for all US time zones + EU) *(reduced window for operational efficiency)*
- **Response Expectations:** 
  - Urgent: 2 hours during business hours
  - Standard: 24 hours for all team members *(simplified from complex EU exceptions)*

---

## 3. EQUIPMENT & WORKSPACE POLICY

### Home Office Setup
**Standard Allocation (Upon Request with Manager Approval):**
- External monitor (company standard model: $200)
- Ergonomic chair (up to $300) *(retained for productivity/health benefits)*
- Noise-canceling headphones/headset (company standard: $80)
- **Total per employee:** $580
- **Program Budget Cap:** $30,000 total *(covers majority of employees with cost control)*

**Eligibility Requirements:**
- Employee works from home minimum 3 days/week
- Manager approval documenting business need
- Equipment request form required

**Role-Specific Additions:**
**Engineering:** Second monitor option (+$200)
**Sales:** Ring light and professional backdrop (+$150)

### Office Equipment
- **Hot-desking system** in existing open office area
- **Assigned desks** available for employees requesting permanent setup
- **Main conference room** bookable via existing calendar system

### Equipment Management
- **Procurement:** Bulk purchasing for cost control
- **EU Team:** Local procurement with expense reimbursement (pre-approval required, up to equivalent USD amounts)
- **Ownership:** Company retains ownership, employee responsible for damage/loss
- **Return Policy:** Equipment must be returned if employment ends or work pattern changes to primarily office-based

## 4. PERFORMANCE EVALUATION APPROACH

### Evaluation Principles
1. **Results-focused:** Performance measured by existing KPIs and OKRs, not hours or location
2. **Equity:** No preference given to office vs. remote work in evaluations
3. **Transparency:** Clear metrics established using current measurement systems

### Performance Metrics Framework

**Engineering:**
- Sprint commitments met (existing Jira tracking)
- Code review participation (GitHub metrics)
- Technical documentation contributions
- Bug resolution time (existing ticketing system)

**Sales:**
- Revenue targets and pipeline metrics (existing CRM)
- Client satisfaction scores
- Pipeline progression rates

**Universal Metrics:**
- Goal achievement (existing OKR system)
- Communication effectiveness during anchor day collaborations
- Cross-functional project contributions

### Evaluation Process
- **Quarterly Check-ins:** Use existing 1:1 format, focus on goal progress and support needs
- **Annual Reviews:** Existing comprehensive evaluation process
- **Location Bias Prevention:** Performance discussions focus solely on measurable outcomes from existing systems
- **EU Accommodation:** Performance conversations scheduled during core hours when possible

---

## 5. TIMEZONE COORDINATION STRATEGY

### Communication Protocols

**Synchronous Communication:**
- **Core Hours:** 1-3 PM ET for all critical US-EU coordination
- **US Teams:** 12-5 PM ET for US-only collaboration as needed
- **Urgent Escalation:** Existing on-call rotation protocols

**Asynchronous Communication:**
- **Documentation First:** All decisions recorded in existing shared drive structure
- **Video Updates:** Weekly async team updates for EU review
- **Decision Making:** 24-hour comment period for non-urgent decisions affecting EU team

### Project Management Adaptations
- **Sprint Planning:** EU input via async feedback before live sessions
- **Code Reviews:** 24-hour SLA to accommodate timezone differences
- **Client Handoffs:** Written handoff protocols in existing project management system

### EU Team Integration
- **Weekly Sync:** One team meeting during 1-3 PM ET core hours
- **Buddy System:** Each EU team member paired with US mentor for cultural/process support
- **Professional Development:** EU team uses existing professional development budget for any US office visits *(eliminates separate quarterly travel budget)*

## KEY DEPARTURES FROM VERSION A (WITH JUSTIFICATIONS):

1. **New Hire Period:** Reduced from 30 days to 2 weeks to prevent office overcrowding while maintaining essential onboarding
2. **Equipment Budget:** Added $30,000 total cap and manager approval requirements to control costs and ensure business justification
3. **Core Hours:** Narrowed from 3 hours to 2 hours (1-3 PM ET) for more focused coordination while maintaining EU inclusion
4. **Meeting Recording:** Limited to training and all-hands only to control storage costs and administrative burden
5. **EU Travel:** Eliminated separate quarterly visit budget, using existing professional development funds for cost control
6. **Review Cycle:** Changed to 6-month pilot for faster iteration and risk management
7. **Capacity Management:** Added explicit office capacity limits and booking requirements to address practical space constraints
8. **Equipment Eligibility:** Added work-from-home frequency requirements to ensure cost-effective allocation

Each change maintains Version A's collaborative intent while adding Version B's operational discipline and cost controls.
