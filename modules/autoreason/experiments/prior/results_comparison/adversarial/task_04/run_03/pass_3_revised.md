## Strategic Foundation

### **Core Thesis**
SecureCode AI will dominate a narrow, defensible niche: **AI-powered code review for U.S. defense contractors and government agencies**. We win through regulatory expertise, security-first architecture, and deep vertical focus rather than broad market appeal.

### **Market Reality**
- **Total addressable market:** ~200 viable prospects
- **Serviceable addressable market:** ~75 active buyers over 3 years
- **Our target:** 15-20 customers generating $3-5M ARR by Year 3
- **Exit strategy:** Acquisition by defense technology integrator or cybersecurity platform

## Single-Focus ICP (Revised)

### **Primary Target: Defense Contractors & Federal Agencies**
**Firmographics:**
- 100-2,000 employees
- Active federal contracts requiring CMMC Level 2+ certification
- 10-50 software developers
- $25M+ annual revenue with government contracts >40% of business

**Technographics:**
- Air-gapped or highly restricted development environments
- Existing on-premise development infrastructure
- Active STIG compliance requirements
- Current manual code review processes taking 3+ days average

**Behavioral Indicators:**
- Recently required to implement CMMC compliance program
- Lost contracts or delayed deliveries due to security vulnerabilities
- Struggling to hire/retain developers due to tooling restrictions
- Executive mandate to improve software quality while maintaining security

**Disqualifiers:**
- Commercial-first companies with <30% government revenue
- Organizations successfully using cloud-based AI development tools
- Companies without active security clearance requirements
- Development teams under 10 engineers

**Probability:** 60%
**Impact:** High
**Mitigation:** 
- Validate market size through direct outreach in first 90 days

- Maintain low fixed cost structure for rapid pivot capability

#### **Risk 2: Large vendor competitive response**
**Probability:** 70% within 24 months
**Impact:** High
**Mitigation:**

- Establish exclusive channel partnerships
- Target acquisition discussions before competitive threats materialize

**Probability:** 40%
**Impact:** Medium
**Mitigation:**

- Develop alternative value propositions (IP protection, audit preparation)
- Maintain flexibility in compliance framework support

#### **Risk 4: Cloud policy restrictions relaxed**
**Probability:** 20%
**Impact:** Very High
**Mitigation:**
- Build relationships with government IT decision makers
- Develop proprietary AI models optimized for defense applications
- Create switching costs through workflow integration
