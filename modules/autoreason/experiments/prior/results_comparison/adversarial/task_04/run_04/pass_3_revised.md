## Executive Summary

CodeGuard AI is the **first AI code review platform designed specifically for enterprises that cannot use cloud-based AI tools** due to regulatory, security, or IP protection requirements. We enable these organizations to achieve AI-powered development productivity while maintaining complete data control and compliance posture.

**Core Business Thesis:** 65% of Fortune 1000 companies are effectively locked out of AI development acceleration due to data sovereignty requirements. This creates a $2.8B addressable market for compliant AI solutions.
