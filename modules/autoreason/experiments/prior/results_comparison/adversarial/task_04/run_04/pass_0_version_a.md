**Response:** "We provide quarterly model updates that you can deploy on your schedule. More importantly, our models are trained specifically on your codebase and coding standards, making them more relevant than generic cloud models."

## What CodeGuard AI Should NEVER Claim

### ❌ Don't Position As:
- **"Better AI than OpenAI/GPT-4"** - Focus on security/compliance benefits, not raw AI capability
- **"Cheaper than cloud solutions"** - We're premium-priced for enterprise value
- **"No-code deployment"** - Enterprise deployment requires planning and expertise
- **"Replacement for all development tools"** - We're specifically focused on code review
- **"100% accuracy"** - No AI system is perfect; emphasize continuous improvement
- **"Works for all company sizes"** - We're specifically built for enterprise needs

### ❌ Avoid These Terms:
- "Revolutionary" or "breakthrough" (overpromises capability)
- "Free trial" (enterprise sales require custom pilots)
- "Works out of the box" (enterprise deployment requires customization)
- "Better than human reviewers" (complement, don't replace human expertise)
