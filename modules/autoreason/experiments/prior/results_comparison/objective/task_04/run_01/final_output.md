## POSITIONING GUARDRAILS

### **Claims We Never Make**

**❌ Prohibited Statements:**
- Performance claims without specific context or conditions
- "100% accuracy" or other absolute accuracy claims
- Direct capability comparisons to specific cloud providers
- "Instant" or "zero-configuration" deployment claims
- ROI guarantees or timeline promises without qualification
- "Military-grade" or similar marketing hyperbole

**✅ Required Qualifications:**
- Model performance varies by codebase and training data
- Deployment timelines depend on infrastructure readiness
- Results based on specific customer implementations
- Ongoing tuning may be required for optimal performance

### **Competitive Comparison Guidelines**

**Safe Comparisons:**
- Architecture differences (on-premise vs. cloud)
- Compliance capabilities and certifications
- Data handling and sovereignty approaches
- Integration patterns and deployment models

**Avoid:**
- Head-to-head speed or accuracy claims
- Feature-by-feature capability comparisons
- Disparaging competitor capabilities or organizations
- Claims about competitor security or compliance gaps
