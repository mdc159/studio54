# Go-to-Market Strategy: Kubernetes CLI Configuration Tool

## Executive Summary

This GTM strategy focuses on converting your existing 5k GitHub stars into revenue through a **dual-track approach**: a freemium SaaS model targeting DevOps teams at scale-up companies, combined with professional services for Kubernetes consultants. With a 3-person team, we'll prioritize the higher-conversion services track while building the SaaS foundation for long-term scalability.

**Key Approach:** Start with professional tooling and services for power users who will pay immediately, while simultaneously building team-focused SaaS features that unlock larger contract values once proven.

## Target Customer Segments

### Primary Segment (Months 1-6): Kubernetes Consultants and Senior Platform Engineers
**Profile:**
- Independent Kubernetes specialists and senior engineers at consultancies
- Platform engineers at Series B+ companies (200-1000 employees)
- Managing multiple complex K8s environments (10+ clusters)
- Personal/team tool budget: $2,000-10,000 annually
- Need advanced tooling to deliver projects faster

**Pain Points:**
- Manually recreating configuration patterns across projects/environments
- Complex policy validation beyond basic YAML checking
- Time spent on config debugging reduces productivity
- Need integration with existing GitOps workflows

**Buying Process:**
- Individual purchase decisions ($299-2,499 range)
- Quick evaluation cycle (1-2 weeks)
- Purchase based on demonstrated time savings
- Often expense as business tool or bill to clients

### Secondary Segment (Months 6-12): DevOps Teams at Scale-up Companies
**Profile:**
- Companies with 50-500 employees, $10M-100M revenue
- DevOps teams of 3-15 engineers managing 10-50 clusters
- Established K8s practices but need better team coordination
- Budget authority for $10K-50K annual tools

**Pain Points:**
- Multi-environment configuration management
- Team coordination and knowledge sharing
- Compliance requirements (SOC2, PCI)
- Onboarding new team members efficiently

**Rationale for Order:** Professional users have immediate budget and pain, providing faster validation and cash flow. Team features require more development but unlock higher ACV once proven.

## Pricing Model

### Hybrid Structure: Professional Tools + Team SaaS

**Open Source Core (Current)**
- CLI tool with basic functionality
- Individual developer use
- Community support via GitHub

**Professional CLI License ($299/year per user)**
- Advanced validation engine with custom rules
- Configuration pattern library (50+ templates)
- Priority support (48-hour email response)
- Commercial use license for consulting
- Integration with Git providers

**Team Collaboration ($149/user/month, 5-user minimum)**
- Web dashboard for configuration sharing and review
- Team-level RBAC and audit logs
- Configuration history and rollback capabilities
- Slack/Teams integrations
- Up to 25 clusters per team

**Enterprise Support Package ($2,499/year per team)**
- Everything in Professional + Team tiers
- Custom validation rule development (8 hours included)
- Integration consultation (16 hours included)
- Priority Slack support with 4-hour SLA
- Unlimited clusters
- On-premises deployment option

**Custom Development Services ($200/hour)**
- Bespoke configuration patterns
- Integration development
- Training workshops and implementation support

**Rationale:** Professional licenses provide immediate revenue from power users. Team tier targets the higher-value segment with SaaS economics. Services offer highest margins and fastest path to revenue.

## What We Will Explicitly NOT Do (Year 1)

### Product Scope
**No Multi-Cloud Strategy:** Kubernetes-only focus until strong market penetration
**No Advanced Analytics/BI:** Core workflow improvements only
**No Mobile Applications:** CLI and web dashboard sufficient
**No White-Label Solutions:** Direct sales model only

### Market Expansion
**No SMB Market (<50 employees):** Insufficient budget and complexity for deal size
**No International Localization:** English-only until $1M ARR
**No Compliance Templates:** Build after enterprise demand proven

### Go-to-Market
**No Paid Advertising:** Organic growth until clear CAC:LTV ratios established
**No Conference Sponsorships:** Attendance and speaking only, no booth sponsorship
**No Complex Channel Partnerships:** Direct relationships until business model proven
**No 24/7 Support:** Business hours sufficient for Year 1 customer base

### Operations
**No Dedicated Sales Hire:** Founder-led sales until $40K+ MRR
**No International Expansion:** Single market focus for simplicity
**No Custom Professional Services Team:** Founders handle consulting until proven demand

**Rationale:** Explicit constraints prevent scope creep and maintain focus on core value delivery with limited resources.
