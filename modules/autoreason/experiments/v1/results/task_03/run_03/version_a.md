# HYBRID WORK POLICY
**Effective Date: [Date]**  
**Version 1.0**

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This policy establishes a flexible hybrid framework that respects departmental needs while maintaining company cohesion. Engineering operates remote-first with minimal office requirements, Sales maintains high in-person collaboration, and all teams follow unified standards for meetings, performance, and equipment.

---

## 1. REQUIRED IN-OFFICE DAYS

### Engineering Team (20 people)
- **Minimum requirement:** 1 day per month per person
- **Team coordination days:** One full-team day per quarter (scheduled 60 days in advance)
- **Flexibility:** Teams may self-organize additional days based on project needs
- **Core collaboration window:** 11 AM - 3 PM PT when in office

### Sales Team (15 people)
- **Minimum requirement:** 3 days per week per person
- **Required days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday (core collaboration days)
- **Flexibility:** Monday/Friday can be remote unless client meetings scheduled
- **Team meetings:** All hands on Wednesdays, 2-4 PM local time

### All Other Departments (15 people)
- **Standard requirement:** 2 days per week per person
- **Recommended days:** Wednesday (company-wide anchor day) + one flexible day
- **Department coordination:** Each department designates one additional team day monthly

### Special Circumstances
- **New hires:** First 30 days require 3 office days/week regardless of department
- **Performance improvement plans:** May require increased office presence (case-by-case)
- **Client-facing roles:** Additional office days required during client visit weeks

---

## 2. MEETING NORMS

### Scheduling Standards
- **Core meeting hours:** 11 AM - 3 PM PT / 2-6 PM ET (accommodates EU morning overlap)
- **EU inclusion rule:** Meetings with EU participants must start by 11 AM PT
- **No-meeting blocks:** 
  - Before 9 AM PT (protects EU evening)
  - After 6 PM ET (protects West Coast morning)
  - Fridays after 2 PM PT (focus time)

### Meeting Types and Protocols

**All-Hands Meetings**
- Monthly, rotating time: 10 AM PT one month, 8 AM PT the next
- Recorded with transcripts for async viewing
- EU team joins alternating months live, watches recording otherwise

**Department Meetings**
- Engineering: Async-first standups, weekly video sync at 12 PM PT
- Sales: Daily 9 AM ET huddles, weekly pipeline reviews Wednesday 2 PM ET
- Other departments: Flexible within core hours

**Cross-Department Collaboration**
- Default to 12-2 PM PT window
- Hybrid meetings require:
  - Professional lighting/audio for remote participants
  - Dedicated meeting facilitator to ensure remote inclusion
  - Shared documents accessible before/during/after
  - 5-minute buffer for technical setup

### Meeting Technology Requirements
- **Primary platform:** [Zoom/Teams/etc.]
- **Room booking:** All office meetings must be scheduled in [calendar system] with video link
- **Documentation:** All decisions recorded in [shared workspace]
- **Action items:** Assigned with owners and due dates in [project management tool]

## 3. EQUIPMENT POLICY

### Standard Remote Setup (All Employees)
**Provided by Company:**
- Laptop (MacBook Pro/equivalent PC)
- External monitor (24" minimum)
- Wireless keyboard and mouse
- Noise-canceling headphones with microphone
- Webcam (if laptop camera insufficient)
- Desk chair (up to $400 reimbursement with receipt)

**Home Office Stipend:**
- $500 one-time setup allowance for new remote workers
- $50/month ongoing internet reimbursement
- $200/year for office supplies and equipment maintenance

### Office Equipment
**Available in All Offices:**
- Hot-desking stations with monitors and peripherals
- Conference rooms with hybrid meeting technology
- Printing and scanning facilities
- Secure storage lockers (daily use only)

**Reserved Equipment (For Regular Office Users):**
- Sales team: Dedicated desks with dual monitors
- Engineering: Bookable quiet zones with specialized development setups

### Equipment Management
- **IT support:** Remote diagnostics within 4 hours, replacement within 24 hours
- **Office equipment issues:** On-site IT support during office hours
- **Replacement cycle:** Laptops every 3 years, peripherals as needed
- **Return policy:** All equipment returned within 5 days of employment termination

## 4. PERFORMANCE EVALUATION APPROACH

### Universal Performance Standards
Performance measured by **outcomes and impact**, not location or hours worked.

### Evaluation Framework

**Quarterly Check-ins (All Employees):**
- Goal achievement and progress metrics
- Collaboration effectiveness (peer feedback)
- Communication quality and responsiveness
- Professional development progress

**Location-Neutral Metrics:**
- **Engineering:** Code quality, sprint completion, technical leadership, knowledge sharing
- **Sales:** Pipeline generation, close rates, client satisfaction, team collaboration
- **All roles:** Project delivery, cross-functional partnership, company value demonstration

### Performance Documentation
**Required for All Employees:**
- Weekly goal updates in [performance management system]
- Quarterly self-assessment with specific examples
- 360-degree feedback (including remote/in-office collaboration)
- Professional development plan with measurable objectives

### Management Training Requirements
**All managers complete:**
- Remote team management certification (within 90 days)
- Bias awareness training focused on location-based assumptions
- Quarterly calibration sessions to ensure consistent standards

### Performance Improvement Process
1. **Early intervention:** Monthly check-ins for struggling performers
2. **Location flexibility:** Underperformers may request schedule adjustments
3. **Support resources:** Coaching, training, and equipment upgrades available
4. **Fair evaluation:** Performance issues addressed through documented behavior/output, never location preference

## 5. TIMEZONE COORDINATION STRATEGY

### Core Business Hours
- **Primary overlap:** 11 AM - 3 PM PT (2-6 PM ET, 7-11 PM GMT)
- **Secondary overlap:** 8-11 AM PT for urgent EU coordination

### Communication Protocols

**Synchronous Communication (Real-time response expected):**
- **Slack/Teams:** During overlap hours only
- **Phone/video calls:** Scheduled in advance with timezone consideration
- **Emergency contact:** Defined escalation path for true urgencies

**Asynchronous Communication (24-48 hour response):**
- **Email:** All non-urgent business communication
- **Project updates:** Posted in shared workspaces with @mentions
- **Documentation:** Comprehensive notes for handoffs between timezones

### EU Team Integration (3 people)
**Special Accommodations:**
- **Meeting rotation:** EU team joins 50% of recurring meetings live, 50% async
- **Decision delays:** 24-hour minimum for decisions affecting EU team
- **Handoff protocols:** Detailed end-of-day summaries for US team pickup
- **Cultural events:** Quarterly virtual EU coffee chats during their morning hours

### Timezone Scheduling Tools
- **Calendar system:** All meetings show multiple timezones
- **Scheduling assistant:** Required for cross-timezone meeting planning  
- **Time zone awareness:** Email signatures include current local time
- **Coverage planning:** Documented backup coverage for each timezone
